# 5.1 商务沟通英语 (Business Communication English)

## 学习目标 Learning Objectives

- 能使用核心商务词汇进行会议、谈判和邮件沟通
- 掌握提出建议、说明观点和澄清信息的表达方式
- 能在商务场景中自信地交流

## 实用句型 Useful Sentences

### 提出建议 (Making Proposals)

| 句型 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| I'd like to propose… | 我想提议…… | **I'd like to propose** a new approach to client communication. |
| I suggest we… | 我建议我们…… | **I suggest we** revise the budget before the next meeting. |
| What if we… | 如果我们……怎么样 | **What if we** collaborate with the marketing team on this? |

### 说明方面 (Specifying Aspects)

| 句型 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| In terms of… | 在……方面 | **In terms of** revenue, this quarter has been very strong. |
| Regarding… | 关于…… | **Regarding** the deadline, we need more time. |
| With respect to… | 关于；就……而言 | **With respect to** your proposal, I have some feedback. |

### 澄清说明 (Clarifying)

| 句型 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| Let me clarify… | 我来澄清…… | **Let me clarify** what I meant by "strategy." |
| To put it simply… | 简单来说…… | **To put it simply**, we need to negotiate a better contract. |
| In other words… | 换句话说…… | **In other words**, the client wants a more detailed presentation. |

## 语法要点 Grammar Point

### 礼貌请求与委婉表达 (Polite Requests & Indirect Language)

在商务沟通中，使用礼貌、委婉的表达非常重要。常用结构：

**1. Would like to — 想要（比 want 更礼貌）**

- I **would like to** propose a change to the agenda.
- We **would like to** request an extension on the deadline.

**2. Could / Would / Might — 情态动词的礼貌用法**

- **Could** you review the contract by Friday?
- **Would** you mind sending me the memo?
- **Might** I suggest an alternative strategy?

**3. I was wondering if — 更委婉的请求**

- I **was wondering if** you could give me feedback on my presentation.
- I **was wondering if** we could reschedule the conference call.

**4. It might be a good idea to… — 温和建议**

- It **might be a good idea to** collaborate with the design team.
- It **might be a good idea to** prepare a backup proposal.

> 在商务场合中，语气往往比内容更重要。使用上述句型可以让你显得更加专业和有礼貌。

## 对话练习 Conversation Practice

**场景：项目预算会议**

- **Alice:** Good morning, everyone. Let's start with the agenda. First item — the budget for Q3.

- **Bob:** Thanks, Alice. **I'd like to propose** a 10% increase in the marketing budget.

- **Alice:** Interesting. **In terms of** expected revenue, do you think that's justified?

- **Bob:** Yes. We have a major **conference** coming up, and we need stronger branding materials. I've prepared a **proposal** that outlines the details.

- **Alice:** **Let me clarify** — is this about the client presentation at the conference, or overall brand strategy?

- **Bob:** Both, actually. I've been talking with the **client**, and they expect a more polished image from us.

- **Alice:** I see. **Regarding the deadline**, when do you need an answer?

- **Bob:** By Friday. We need to **negotiate** the booth space before it's taken.

- **Alice:** Understood. Let's **collaborate** on this. Tom, can you review the proposal from a financial perspective?

- **Tom:** Sure. I'll have **feedback** for you by Thursday.

- **Alice:** Great. Let's build strong **rapport** with the client through this project. Moving on to the next item on the **agenda**…

> **注意观察**：对话中使用了 I'd like to propose, In terms of, Let me clarify, Regarding the deadline 等句型，以及 negotiate, proposal, conference, client, budget 等核心词汇。

## 自测练习 Self-Test

**1. 选择正确的词汇填空。**

a) We need to \_\_\_\_\_\_ the terms of the agreement before signing. (negotiate / feedback)

b) Please send me the meeting \_\_\_\_\_\_ so I know what will be discussed. (memo / agenda)

c) The \_\_\_\_\_\_ for submitting the proposal is next Monday. (deadline / revenue)

d) Building \_\_\_\_\_\_ with new clients takes time and effort. (rapport / budget)

e) Our \_\_\_\_\_\_ for this quarter exceeded expectations. (strategy / revenue)

**2. 将下列句子改为更礼貌的表达。**

a) I want to change the plan. → I \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ to change the plan.

b) Give me your feedback by Friday. → \_\_\_\_\_\_ you give me your feedback by Friday?

c) You should try a different strategy. → It \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ a good idea to try a different strategy.

**3. 选择正确的句型完成句子。**

a) \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ budget, we have some concerns about the proposed increase.
- A) In terms of
- B) Let me clarify
- C) I'd like to propose

b) \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ that we hire a consultant for the new project.
- A) In terms of
- B) I'd like to propose
- C) Let me clarify

**4. 翻译以下句子为英文。**

a) 我想提议改变我们的会议议程。
b) 在客户满意度方面，我们做得很好。
c) 让我澄清一下我的意思。**答案：**

1. a) negotiate, b) agenda, c) deadline, d) rapport, e) revenue

2. a) I would like to change the plan. / I'd like to change the plan.
   b) Could you give me your feedback by Friday?
   c) It might be a good idea to try a different strategy.

3. a) A (In terms of), b) B (I'd like to propose)

4. a) I'd like to propose changing our meeting agenda.
   b) In terms of client satisfaction, we are doing very well.
   c) Let me clarify what I mean.
