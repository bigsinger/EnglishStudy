# 语法快速参考 Grammar Reference

> 本篇为华尔街英语自学教程附录，系统梳理英语语法核心知识点，涵盖Pre-A1至C1级别。每个语法点配1-2个例句帮助理解。## 一、词法 Parts of Speech

### 名词 Nouns

**可数名词与不可数名词 (Countable & Uncountable Nouns)**

可数名词有单复数形式，可以用冠词 a/an 以及数词修饰；不可数名词没有复数形式，不能与 a/an 直接连用。

- **可数名词 (Countable)**：可以用数字直接计数，有单数和复数之分。
  - 单数：a book, an apple, one car
  - 复数：two books, three apples, many cars
  - *She bought **a book** yesterday.*
  - *There are **three apples** on the table.*

- **不可数名词 (Uncountable)**：无法用数字直接计数，通常表示物质、抽象概念或集合。
  - 常见不可数名词：water, information, advice, money, furniture, news, homework, music
  - 表示数量需用量词：a glass of water, a piece of advice, a lot of furniture
  - *Can I have **some water**?*
  - *He gave me **a useful piece of advice**.*

**名词单复数变化规则 (Plural Forms)**

- 一般加 -s：book → books, cat → cats
- 以 s, x, ch, sh 结尾加 -es：bus → buses, box → boxes, watch → watches, dish → dishes
- 以辅音+y 结尾变 y 为 i 加 -es：city → cities, baby → babies
- 以 f/fe 结尾变 f 为 v 加 -es：knife → knives, leaf → leaves（少数直接加 -s：roof → roofs）
- 不规则变化：man → men, woman → women, child → children, foot → feet, tooth → teeth, mouse → mice, sheep → sheep, deer → deer
  - *The **children** are playing in the park.*
  - *I saw two **mice** in the kitchen.*### 代词 Pronouns

**人称代词 (Personal Pronouns)**

| 人称 | 主格 (主语) | 宾格 (宾语) |
|------|------------|------------|
| 第一人称单数 | I | me |
| 第二人称单数 | you | you |
| 第三人称单数 | he/she/it | him/her/it |
| 第一人称复数 | we | us |
| 第二人称复数 | you | you |
| 第三人称复数 | they | them |

- ***She** loves **him**.*（她爱他。）
- ***They** invited **us** to the party.*（他们邀请了我们参加派对。）

**物主代词 (Possessive Pronouns)**

形容词性物主代词后必须接名词，名词性物主代词单独使用。

- 形容词性：my, your, his, her, its, our, their
- 名词性：mine, yours, his, hers, its, ours, theirs
- *This is **my** book. = This book is **mine**.*
- *Your car is red, but **mine** is blue.*

**反身代词 (Reflexive Pronouns)**

主语和宾语为同一人时使用，也用于强调。

- myself, yourself, himself, herself, itself, ourselves, yourselves, themselves
- *I taught **myself** English.*（我自学英语。）
- *He hurt **himself** while cooking.*（他做饭时伤了自己。）

**指示代词 (Demonstrative Pronouns)**

- this（这个，近处单数）/ these（这些，近处复数）
- that（那个，远处单数）/ those（那些，远处复数）
- ***This** is my phone, and **that** is yours.*
- ***These** books are interesting, but **those** are boring.*### 动词 Verbs

**及物动词与不及物动词 (Transitive & Intransitive Verbs)**

- **及物动词**：后接宾语，动作作用于某物或某人。
  - *She **read** a novel.*（read 后接宾语 a novel）
  - *He **bought** a gift for his mother.*

- **不及物动词**：不接宾语，或接介词后再接宾语。
  - *The baby **cried**.*（cried 后无宾语）
  - *She **listened** to the music.*（listened 需加 to 再接宾语）

- 有些动词既可及物又可不及物：eat, read, write, sing
  - *He **ate** (vi.) quickly. / He **ate** an apple (vt.).*

**系动词 (Linking Verbs)**

系动词连接主语和主语补足语（表语），表示状态或变化。

- **状态系动词**：be（am/is/are/was/were）
  - *She **is** a teacher.*
  - *They **are** happy.*

- **感官系动词**：look, sound, smell, taste, feel
  - *This soup **tastes** delicious.*
  - *You **look** tired.*

- **变化系动词**：become, get, grow, turn, go, come
  - *It **got** dark outside.*
  - *He **became** a doctor.*### 形容词 Adjectives

**形容词的位置与顺序 (Position & Order)**

- **位置**：形容词通常放在名词之前（定语用法）或系动词之后（表语用法）。
  - *It is a **beautiful** day.*（定语）
  - *The day is **beautiful**.*（表语）

- **多个形容词的顺序**（OSASCOMP 规则）：
  1. 观点/评价 (Opinion)：beautiful, ugly, nice
  2. 尺寸 (Size)：big, small, huge
  3. 年龄 (Age)：old, young, new
  4. 形状 (Shape)：round, square, flat
  5. 颜色 (Color)：red, blue, green
  6. 产地 (Origin)：Chinese, American, Japanese
  7. 材料 (Material)：wooden, silk, plastic
  8. 用途 (Purpose)：sleeping (bag), dining (table)
  - *She bought a **beautiful small old round Chinese wooden dining table**.*
  - *He lives in a **nice big new house**.*

**比较级与最高级 (Comparative & Superlative)**

- **单音节和部分双音节词**：加 -er / -est
  - tall → **taller** → **tallest**
  - *He is **taller** than his brother.*
  - *She is the **tallest** in the class.*

- **多音节词**：加 more / most
  - beautiful → **more beautiful** → **most beautiful**
  - *This painting is **more beautiful** than that one.*
  - *This is the **most beautiful** painting I've ever seen.*

- **不规则变化**：
  - good/well → better → best
  - bad/badly → worse → worst
  - many/much → more → most
  - little → less → least
  - far → farther/further → farthest/furthest
  - *Your English is **better** than mine.*
  - *This is the **worst** movie I've ever watched.*### 副词 Adverbs

**频率副词 (Adverbs of Frequency)**

表示动作发生的频率，通常放在行为动词之前、be 动词之后。

- always (100%) → usually (80%) → often (60%) → sometimes (40%) → occasionally (20%) → seldom/hardly ever (5%) → never (0%)
- *She **always** gets up early.*
- *I **sometimes** eat out on weekends.*

**方式副词 (Adverbs of Manner)**

描述动作进行的方式，通常以 -ly 结尾，放在动词之后或宾语之后。

- quickly, slowly, carefully, badly, well, loudly, quietly
- *She sings **beautifully**.*
- *Please drive **carefully** on this road.*

**程度副词 (Adverbs of Degree)**

修饰形容词或其他副词，表示程度。

- very, quite, rather, fairly, extremely, too, enough, hardly, almost
- *The movie was **very** interesting.*
- *It's **too** hot to go outside.*

**时间副词 (Adverbs of Time)**

表示动作发生的时间，通常放在句末或句首。

- now, then, today, yesterday, tomorrow, soon, recently, already, yet, still
- *I have **already** finished my homework.*
- ***Yesterday**, we went to the museum.*### 介词 Prepositions

**时间介词 (Prepositions of Time)**

- **at**：具体时间点（at 8 o'clock, at noon, at night, at midnight）
  - *The meeting starts **at** 9 AM.*

- **in**：较长的时间段（in the morning, in April, in 2024, in summer, in two hours）
  - *She was born **in** 1995.*

- **on**：具体的某一天（on Monday, on June 1st, on Christmas Day）
  - *We'll meet **on** Friday.*

- **其他时间介词**：before / after / since / for / during / until / by
  - *I've worked here **since** 2020.*（since + 时间点）
  - *She studied **for** three hours.*（for + 时间段）

**地点介词 (Prepositions of Place)**

- **at**：具体位置或地点（at the door, at the bus stop, at home）
  - *I'll wait for you **at** the station.*

- **in**：在内部或较大范围（in the room, in the city, in the world）
  - *The book is **in** my bag.*

- **on**：在表面或某条线上（on the table, on the wall, on the page）
  - *There's a cup **on** the desk.*

- **其他地点介词**：under / above / between / next to / behind / in front of / near
  - *The cat is sleeping **under** the bed.*
  - *Our school is **next to** the library.*

**方向介词 (Prepositions of Direction)**

- **to**：朝向某处
  - *She went **to** the supermarket.*

- **into**：进入（从外到内）
  - *He walked **into** the room.*

- **onto**：到…上面
  - *The cat jumped **onto** the sofa.*

- **across / through / along / up / down / towards / from**
  - *We walked **across** the bridge.*
  - *They drove **through** the tunnel.*### 连词 Conjunctions

**并列连词 (Coordinating Conjunctions)**

连接平行结构的词、短语或分句。常见的有 FANBOYS（for, and, nor, but, or, yet, so）。

- **and**（并列）：*I like coffee **and** tea.*
- **but**（转折）：*He is rich **but** unhappy.*
- **or**（选择）：*Would you like tea **or** coffee?*
- **so**（结果）：*It was raining, **so** we stayed home.*
- **yet**（然而）：*She is tired **yet** happy.*
- **for**（因为）：*He left early, **for** he had a meeting.*
- **nor**（也不）：*He doesn't like it, **nor** do I.*

**从属连词 (Subordinating Conjunctions)**

引导从句，连接主句和从句，表示时间、原因、条件、让步等关系。

- **时间**：when, while, before, after, as soon as, until, since
  - *Call me **when** you arrive.*

- **原因**：because, since, as
  - *I stayed home **because** I was sick.*

- **条件**：if, unless, as long as, provided that
  - *You will pass **if** you study hard.*

- **让步**：although, even though, though, while
  - ***Although** it rained, we went hiking.*

- **目的**：so that, in order that
  - *She spoke loudly **so that** everyone could hear.*

- **结果**：so...that, such...that
  - *He was **so** tired **that** he fell asleep immediately.*

- **比较**：than, as...as
  - *She is **as** smart **as** her sister.*## 二、时态 Tenses

### 一般现在时 Present Simple

**形式**：主语 + 动词原形（第三人称单数加 -s/-es）
**用法**：
- 表示习惯性/反复发生的动作：*I **get up** at 7 every morning.*
- 表示客观事实或普遍真理：*The sun **rises** in the east.*
- 表示状态、感觉、拥有等：*She **likes** classical music.*

**否定句**：主语 + don't/doesn't + 动词原形
- *He **doesn't speak** French.*

**疑问句**：Do/Does + 主语 + 动词原形？
- ***Do** you **like** pizza?*

### 现在进行时 Present Continuous

**形式**：am/is/are + 现在分词（-ing）
**用法**：
- 说话时正在进行的动作：*Look! It **is raining** outside.*
- 现阶段正在进行的动作（不一定此刻）：*I **am reading** a very interesting book these days.*
- 按计划安排将要发生的动作（近期将来）：*We **are leaving** tomorrow.*

**否定句**：am/is/are + not + -ing
- *She **is not working** today.*

**疑问句**：Am/Is/Are + 主语 + -ing？
- ***Are** you **listening** to me?*

### 现在完成时 Present Perfect

**形式**：have/has + 过去分词
**用法**：
- 过去发生但对现在有影响的动作：*I **have lost** my keys.*（现在找不到钥匙了）
- 从过去持续到现在的动作或状态：*She **has lived** here for ten years.*
- 表示"曾经/从未"的经历：*He **has never been** to Japan.*

**已发生/未发生**：already（肯定句）, yet（否定句/疑问句）, ever（曾经）, never（从未）
- *I have **already** finished my homework.*
- *Have you **ever** been to Paris?*

### 现在完成进行时 Present Perfect Continuous

**形式**：have/has + been + 现在分词（-ing）
**用法**：
- 从过去持续到现在并且仍在继续的动作（强调持续性）：*I **have been waiting** for an hour.*
- 刚刚结束的动作（刚完成且有可见结果）：*You look tired. **Have you been running**?*

**对比**：现在完成时强调结果，现在完成进行时强调过程。
- *I **have written** three emails.*（结果：三封邮件写好了）
- *I **have been writing** emails all morning.*（过程：整个上午都在写）

### 一般过去时 Past Simple

**形式**：主语 + 动词过去式（规则动词加 -ed，不规则动词需记忆）
**用法**：
- 过去某个时间发生的动作或状态：*I **visited** Beijing last year.*
- 过去习惯性动作：*When I was young, I **played** basketball every day.*

**否定句**：主语 + didn't + 动词原形
- *They **didn't go** to the party.*

**疑问句**：Did + 主语 + 动词原形？
- ***Did** you **see** the movie?*

### 过去进行时 Past Continuous

**形式**：was/were + 现在分词（-ing）
**用法**：
- 过去某一时刻正在进行的动作：*I **was watching** TV at 8 last night.*
- 过去某一时间段内持续进行的动作：*They **were building** a house last year.*
- 一个动作发生时另一个动作正在进行（长动作用进行时，短动作用一般过去时）：*While I **was walking** home, I **met** an old friend.*

### 过去完成时 Past Perfect

**形式**：had + 过去分词
**用法**：
- 表示"过去的过去"——在过去某个时间点之前已经完成的动作：*When we arrived, the movie **had already started**.*
- 常用于间接引语中：*She said she **had finished** her work.*

### 一般将来时 Future Simple

**will + 动词原形**：
- 临时决定：*I **will help** you carry that.*（说话时刚做的决定）
- 预测（基于主观看法）：*I think it **will rain** tomorrow.*
- 承诺/威胁：*I **will call** you later.*

**be going to + 动词原形**：
- 计划/intention：*We **are going to visit** London next summer.*
- 预测（基于现有证据）：*Look at those dark clouds! It **is going to rain**.*

### 将来进行时 Future Continuous

**形式**：will be + 现在分词（-ing）
**用法**：
- 将来某一时刻正在进行的动作：*This time next week, I **will be lying** on the beach.*
- 按计划安排将来会发生的事：*The professor **will be giving** a lecture at 2 PM.*

### 将来完成时 Future Perfect

**形式**：will have + 过去分词
**用法**：
- 将来某一时间点之前已经完成的动作：*By next March, I **will have finished** my thesis.*
- *When you arrive, the train **will have left**.*

### 时态用法总结

各时态的核心时间概念：

| 时态 | 核心含义 | 关键词提示 |
|------|---------|-----------|
| 一般现在时 | 习惯、事实、状态 | always, usually, every day |
| 现在进行时 | 此刻或现阶段正在进行 | now, at the moment, currently |
| 现在完成时 | 过去→现在，强调结果/经历 | already, yet, ever, never, just |
| 现在完成进行时 | 过去→现在，强调持续过程 | all morning, for hours, since |
| 一般过去时 | 过去已结束的动作 | yesterday, last week, ago |
| 过去进行时 | 过去某刻正在做 | at that time, while |
| 过去完成时 | 过去的过去 | before, by the time, already |
| 一般将来时 | 将来要发生 | tomorrow, next week, soon |
| 将来进行时 | 将来某刻正在做 | this time tomorrow |
| 将来完成时 | 将来某时之前已完成 | by next year, by then |

- *The train **leaves** at 6 PM tomorrow.*（一般现在时表按时刻表发生的将来动作）
- *I **will have been working** here for 20 years next fall.*（将来完成进行时）## 三、句式 Sentence Structures

### 简单句 / 并列句 / 复合句

**简单句 (Simple Sentence)**：只有一个主谓结构。
- *She **reads** books.*
- *The **sun** **shines** brightly.*

**并列句 (Compound Sentence)**：两个或以上并列分句，由并列连词连接（and, but, or, so, yet）。
- *I wanted to go, **but** I felt too tired.*
- *She studied hard, **so** she passed the exam.*

**复合句 (Complex Sentence)**：一个主句加一个或多个从句，由从属连词连接。
- *I **will call you** when **I arrive**.*
- ***Although** it was late, **he continued working**.*

### 疑问句 Questions

**一般疑问句 (Yes/No Questions)**：用 Yes 或 No 回答。
- ***Do** you like pizza? — Yes, I do. / No, I don't.*
- ***Is** she your sister?*

**特殊疑问句 (Wh- Questions)**：以疑问词开头，询问具体信息。
- 疑问词：what, where, when, why, who, whom, whose, which, how
- *What is your name?*
- *Where do you live?*
- *Why are you late?*
- *How long have you been studying English?*

**选择疑问句 (Alternative Questions)**：提供两个或多个选项，用 or 连接。
- *Would you like tea **or** coffee?*
- *Are you going by bus **or** by train?*

**反义疑问句 (Tag Questions)**：陈述句 + 简短问句，前肯后否或前否后肯。
- *You are a student, **aren't you**?*
- *She doesn't like coffee, **does she**?*
- 注意：*Let's go, **shall we**?*  /  *Open the door, **will you**?*

### 祈使句 / 感叹句

**祈使句 (Imperative Sentences)**：表示命令、请求、建议，动词原形开头，主语 you 通常省略。
- ***Sit** down, please.*
- ***Don't** smoke here.*
- ***Let's** go for a walk.*

**感叹句 (Exclamatory Sentences)**：用 what 或 how 引导，表达强烈情感。
- *What a beautiful **day** (it is)!*（What + 名词短语）
- *What **nice pictures** (they are)!*
- ***How** beautiful the **flower** is!*（How + 形容词/副词 + 主语 + 谓语）
- ***How** fast he **runs**!*

### 条件句 Conditionals

**零条件句 (Zero Conditional)**：客观事实或必然结果（if 从句用一般现在时，主句也用一般现在时）。
- ***If** you heat ice, it **melts**.*
- ***When** it rains, the ground **gets** wet.*

**第一条件句 (First Conditional)**：将来可能发生的真实情况（if 从句用一般现在时，主句用 will + 动词原形）。
- ***If** it rains tomorrow, I **will take** an umbrella.*
- ***If** you study hard, you **will pass** the exam.*

**第二条件句 (Second Conditional)**：与现在事实相反的假设或将来不太可能的情况（if 从句用一般过去时，主句用 would + 动词原形）。
- ***If** I **had** a million dollars, I **would travel** around the world.*（实际上没有）
- ***If** I **were** you, I **would accept** the offer.*（实际上我不可能是你）

**第三条件句 (Third Conditional)**：与过去事实相反的假设（if 从句用过去完成时，主句用 would have + 过去分词）。
- ***If** I **had studied** harder, I **would have passed** the exam.*（实际上没通过）
- ***If** he **had left** earlier, he **would have caught** the train.*（实际上没赶上）

**混合条件句 (Mixed Conditional)**：从句与主句时间不一致的假设，最常见的是从句指过去、主句指现在。
- ***If** I **had taken** that job (过去), I **would be** rich now (现在).*
- ***If** she **hadn't missed** the flight (过去), she **would be** here now (现在).*

### 虚拟语气 Subjunctive Mood

虚拟语气用于表达愿望、建议、要求、命令等非真实情况。

-**wish** 后的从句：
- 与现在事实相反（一般过去时）：*I wish I **knew** the answer.*（可惜我不知道）
- 与过去事实相反（过去完成时）：*I wish I **had studied** harder.*（可惜没努力）
- 将来的愿望（would + 动词原形）：*I wish it **would stop** raining.*

-**as if / as though**：仿佛...
- *She talks **as if** she **knew** everything.*（一般过去时，与现在事实相反）
- *He looked **as if** he **had seen** a ghost.*（过去完成时，与过去事实相反）

-**It is time that...**：该做某事了（从句用一般过去时）
- *It's time we **left**.*
- *It's high time you **got** a job.*

**建议/要求类动词**后的 that 从句用 (should +) 动词原形：
- suggest, recommend, insist, demand, propose, order
- *I suggest that he **go** to the doctor.*
- *They recommended that the plan **be** changed.*

### 被动语态 Passive Voice

**形式**：be + 过去分词（by + 动作发出者）

各时态的被动语态：

- **一般现在时**：am/is/are + 过去分词
  - *English **is spoken** all over the world.*
  - *These books **are sold** at many stores.*

- **现在进行时**：am/is/are + being + 过去分词
  - *The road **is being repaired** right now.*

- **现在完成时**：have/has + been + 过去分词
  - *The report **has been finished**.*

- **一般过去时**：was/were + 过去分词
  - *This bridge **was built** in 1920.*

- **过去进行时**：was/were + being + 过去分词
  - *The house **was being painted** when I arrived.*

- **过去完成时**：had + been + 过去分词
  - *The work **had been completed** before the deadline.*

- **一般将来时**：will be + 过去分词
  - *The results **will be announced** next week.*

- **情态动词被动**：情态动词 + be + 过去分词
  - *This task **must be finished** by Friday.*
  - *The window **can be opened** easily.*

- **带双宾语的动词被动**（如 give, send, show）：
  - 主动：*She gave **me** **a book**.*
  - 被动1：*I **was given** a book (by her).*（间接宾语作主语）
  - 被动2：*A book **was given to me** (by her).*（直接宾语作主语）## 四、从句 Clauses

### 定语从句 Relative Clauses

定语从句修饰名词或代词，由关系代词（who, whom, which, that, whose）或关系副词（where, when, why）引导。

**限制性定语从句 (Restrictive Relative Clauses)**：对先行词进行限定，去掉后句子意思不完整。不用逗号隔开。

- *The woman **who lives next door** is a doctor.*（限定是哪位女士）
- *The book **that I borrowed from you** is wonderful.*
- *This is the house **where I grew up**.*
- *Do you remember the day **when we first met**?*

**非限制性定语从句 (Non-Restrictive Relative Clauses)**：对先行词提供补充信息，去掉后句子意思仍然完整。用逗号隔开，不能用 that。

- *My mother, **who is a teacher**, loves reading.*（补充说明母亲是老师）
- *Paris, **where I visited last year**, is beautiful.*
- *The Eiffel Tower, **which is one of the most famous landmarks in the world**, attracts millions of tourists every year.*

- **注意**：关系代词在从句中作宾语时可省略（限制性定语从句中）：*The book ( **that/which** ) I read last week was fascinating.*### 名词性从句 Noun Clauses

名词性从句在整个句子中充当名词的角色，可以做主语、宾语、表语或同位语。

**主语从句 (Subject Clause)**：从句作句子主语，常以 that/whether/wh- 引导。
- ***That he passed the exam** surprised everyone.*
- ***Whether we will go** depends on the weather.*
- ***What she said** is true.*

**宾语从句 (Object Clause)**：从句作动词或介词的宾语。
- *I think **that he is right**.*
- *She doesn't know **what she wants**.*
- *I'm interested in **what you said**.*

**表语从句 (Predicative Clause)**：从句位于系动词之后，作表语。
- *The problem is **that we don't have enough time**.*
- *That's **what I believe**.*

**同位语从句 (Appositive Clause)**：从句解释说明抽象名词的内容（如 fact, news, idea, hope, belief, thought, question, possibility）。
- *The fact **that he won the award** surprised everyone.*（fact 的内容）
- *I heard the news **that our team had won**.*（news 的内容）
- *There is no doubt **that she is the best candidate**.*### 状语从句 Adverbial Clauses

状语从句修饰主句的动词、形容词或副词，表示时间、原因、条件、让步、目的、结果等关系。

**时间状语从句 (Time Clauses)**

引导词：when, while, as, before, after, until, as soon as, since, whenever
- *Call me **when you arrive**.*
- *She watched TV **while I was cooking**.*
- *I'll wait **until you finish**.*

**原因状语从句 (Reason/Cause Clauses)**

引导词：because, since, as, now that
- *He stayed home **because he was sick**.*
- ***Since you're tired**, let's take a break.*

**条件状语从句 (Conditional Clauses)**（见条件句部分）

引导词：if, unless, as long as, provided that, in case
- *I'll go **unless it rains**.*
- *You can borrow my car **as long as you drive carefully**.*

**让步状语从句 (Concessive Clauses)**

引导词：although, even though, though, while, whereas, no matter, whatever, however
- ***Although it was cold**, she wore a dress.*
- *No **matter what you say**, I won't change my mind.*

**目的状语从句 (Purpose Clauses)**

引导词：so that, in order that, for fear that, in case
- *She spoke slowly **so that everyone could understand**.*
- *I left early **in order that I might catch the first train**.*

**结果状语从句 (Result Clauses)**

引导词：so...that, such...that, so that
- *He was **so tired** **that** he fell asleep at his desk.*
- *It was **such a hot day** **that** we stayed indoors all afternoon.*## 五、其他 Other Topics

### 情态动词 Modal Verbs

情态动词表示可能、能力、必要、推测、意愿等语气，后接动词原形，没有人称和数的变化。

**can / could**
- **能力**：*I **can** swim.*（现在）/ *I **could** swim when I was five.*（过去）
- **请求/许可**：***Can** I use your phone? / **Could** you help me?*（could 更委婉）
- **可能性**：*It **can** get very hot here in summer.*（理论可能）
- **否定推测**：*That **can't** be true.*（不可能是真的）

**may / might**
- **请求/许可**：***May** I come in?*（正式）/ *You **may** leave now.*
- **可能性**：*It **may** rain later.*（大概50%可能）/ *It **might** snow tonight.*（可能性更低）
- **May...但可能不...**：*He **may** **not** come to the party.*

**must**
- **必须**（主观义务）：*You **must** finish this by tomorrow.*
- **否定**：mustn't（禁止）：*You **mustn't** smoke here.*（严禁吸烟）
- **推测（肯定）**：*He **must** be at home. I see his car outside.*（一定在）

**have to**（客观义务）比 must 更具客观性
- *I **have to** wear a uniform at work.*（客观规定）

**should / ought to**
- **建议/义务**：*You **should** see a doctor.*
- **推测（按理应该）**：*They **should** be here by now.*

**would**
- **礼貌请求**：*Would you like some tea?*
- **过去习惯**：*When I was young, I **would** visit my grandparents every summer.*
- **虚拟语气**：*If I had more time, I **would** travel more.*

**情态动词 + have done**（对过去的推测或虚拟）：
- must have done（一定做过了）：*She **must have forgotten** about the meeting.*
- can't have done（不可能做过）：*He **can't have left** because his car is still here.*
- could have done（本来可以做但没做）：*You **could have told** me earlier.*
- should have done（本应该做但没做）：*I **should have studied** harder.*
- might have done（可能做过/本可能做）：*She **might have missed** the bus.*### 非谓语动词 Non-finite Verbs

非谓语动词包括不定式、动名词和分词，不能在句子中单独充当谓语。

**不定式 (Infinitive)**

形式：to + 动词原形

用法：
- **作主语**：*To learn English **is** important.*（常用形式主语 it：*It is important **to learn English**.*）
- **作宾语**：*I want **to travel** abroad.*
- **作定语**：*I have a lot of work **to do**.*
- **作状语（表目的）**：*She went to the library **to study**.*
- **作宾补**：*He asked me **to help** him.*

**动词 + 不定式**（常见）：want, decide, hope, plan, promise, refuse, learn, agree, afford, manage, fail
- *I hope **to see** you soon.*

**动名词 (Gerund)**

形式：动词 + -ing（与现在分词同形，但功能不同）

用法：
- **作主语**：***Swimming** is good for health.*
- **作宾语**：*I enjoy **listening** to music.* / *He is good at **playing** basketball.*
- **作表语**：*Her favorite hobby is **reading**.*

**动词 + 动名词**（常见）：enjoy, finish, avoid, suggest, mind, practice, keep, quit, consider, admit, deny
- *Would you mind **opening** the window?*

**动名词与不定式作宾语的区别**：
- stop doing（停止做某事）：*He stopped **smoking**.*（戒烟了）
- stop to do（停下来去做某事）：*He stopped **to smoke**.*（停下来去抽烟）
- remember doing（记得做过）：*I remember **locking** the door.*（记得锁过了）
- remember to do（记得去做）：*Please remember **to lock** the door.*（别忘了锁门）

**分词 (Participles)**

**现在分词 (Present Participle / -ing)**：表示主动或进行
**过去分词 (Past Participle / -ed / 不规则)**：表示被动或完成

用法：
- **作定语**：
  - *a **boring** movie*（令人无聊的电影）/ *a **bored** student*（感到无聊的学生）
  - *boiling water*（正在沸腾的水）/ *boiled water*（烧开过的水）
- **作状语**：
  - ***Walking** down the street, I met an old friend.*（主动，同时发生）
  - ***Exhausted** by the long journey, he went straight to bed.*（被动/完成）
- **独立主格结构**：*The work **done**, we went home.*（工作做完后，我们回家了。）### 直接引语与间接引语 Direct & Indirect Speech

**直接引语 (Direct Speech)**：一字不差引用原话，加引号。
- *She said, "**I am tired**."*

**间接引语 (Indirect / Reported Speech)**：转述别人说的话，不用引号，需进行人称、时态、时间状语的调整。

**时态变化规则**（主句动词为过去时）：

| 直接引语 | 间接引语 |
|----------|---------|
| 一般现在时 | 一般过去时 |
| 现在进行时 | 过去进行时 |
| 现在完成时 | 过去完成时 |
| 一般过去时 | 过去完成时 |
| 过去进行时 | 过去完成进行时（或不变） |
| will | would |
| can | could |
| may | might |
| must | had to / must |

- 直接：*"I **am** happy," she said.*
- 间接：*She said she **was** happy.*

- 直接：*"I **will** call you," he promised.*
- 间接：*He promised he **would** call me.*

**时间/地点状语变化**：
- now → then / at that time
- today → that day
- yesterday → the day before / the previous day
- tomorrow → the next day / the following day
- here → there
- this → that / these → those

- 直接：*"I will do it **tomorrow**," he said.*
- 间接：*He said he would do it **the next day**.*

**疑问句的间接引语**：变为陈述语序，用 if/whether 或 wh- 连接。
- 直接：*"**Do you like** coffee?" she asked.*
- 间接：*She asked **if I liked** coffee.*

- 直接：*"**Where do you** live?" he asked.*
- 间接：*He asked **where I lived**.*

**祈使句的间接引语**：用 tell/ask/order + 宾语 + to do。
- 直接：*"Please sit down," she said.*
- 间接：*She asked me **to sit down**.*

- 直接：*"Don't smoke," he said.*
- 间接：*He told me **not to smoke**.*### 倒装句 Inversion

倒装句将主语和谓语的位置交换，用于强调或平衡句子结构。

**完全倒装 (Full Inversion)**：谓语全部提到主语之前，常用于表示方位的副词或介词短语开头的句子，主语为名词（非代词）。

- *Here **comes the bus**.*（bus来了）
- *There **goes the last train**.*
- ***On the table stood** a beautiful vase.*
- ***Out ran** the children.*

**部分倒装 (Partial Inversion)**：将助动词/情态动词提到主语前，行为动词位置不变。

- **否定词/半否定词开头的句子**：never, seldom, rarely, hardly, scarcely, nowhere, little
  - ***Never** have I seen such a beautiful sunset.*
  - ***Hardly** had I arrived when the phone rang.*（我刚刚到，电话就响了。）

- **Only + 状语/状语从句开头**：
  - ***Only** then did I realize my mistake.*
  - ***Only** by working hard can you succeed.*

- **Not until... 开头**：
  - ***Not until** she arrived did he start the meeting.*

- **So/Such...that 句型中，so/such 在句首**：
  - ***So** tired was she that she fell asleep immediately.*
  - ***Such** a nice person is he that everyone likes him.*

- **让步状语从句倒装**：*as / though* 引导的让步从句
  - ***Young as** he is, he is very responsible.*（虽然他年轻，但很负责。）
  - ***Try as** I might, I couldn't solve the problem.*

- **虚拟条件句省略 if**：将 had / should / were 提前
  - ***Had** I known, I would have come earlier.*（如果我知道，我早就来了。）
  - ***Were** she here, she would help us.*### 强调句 Emphasis

**It is/was...that/who... (分裂句)**

这是英语中最常用的强调结构，强调句子中除谓语动词外的任意成分。

结构：It is/was + 被强调部分 + that/who + 句子剩余部分

- 原句：*Tom bought a car yesterday.*
- 强调主语：*It **was Tom who** bought a car yesterday.*（是汤姆昨天买了一辆车。）
- 强调宾语：*It **was a car that** Tom bought yesterday.*（汤姆昨天买的是一辆车。）
- 强调时间：*It **was yesterday that** Tom bought a car.*（是在昨天汤姆买了一辆车。）

- *It **is English that** I want to learn most.*（我最想学的是英语。）

**What 引导的名词性从句强调**

- *What I need **is** a good rest.*（我需要的是好好休息一下。）
- *What impressed me most **was** her kindness.*（最让我印象深刻的是她的善良。）

**do/does/did + 动词原形**（强调谓语动词）

- *I **do** believe you.*（我确实相信你。）
- *She **does** work hard.*（她确实工作很努力。）
- *He **did** call you yesterday.*（他昨天确实给你打电话了。）> **上一篇**：[词汇篇 → 阅读与核心词汇] → (../../part6/sections/6.5.md)
