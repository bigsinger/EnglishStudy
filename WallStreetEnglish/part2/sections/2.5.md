# 2.5 简单问答与请求 (Simple Questions & Answers)

> 对应华尔街英语 Level 5 | CEFR A1

## 学习目标 Learning Objectives

- ✅ 掌握6个特殊疑问词（what, where, when, who, why, how）
- ✅ 学会用 Can / May 提出请求
- ✅ 学会给出肯定和否定回答
- ✅ 学会用 please 礼貌表达

### 词汇小贴士

- **Wh-疑问词记忆法**：六个特殊疑问词可以记成"5W1H"——What, Where, When, Who, Why + How。
- **can vs may**：两者都表示"可以"，但 can 更口语化，may 更正式。和朋友说话用 can，和长辈或陌生人说话用 may 更礼貌。
- **please 的位置**：可以在句首（Please help me.）或句尾（Help me, please.），句尾时通常加逗号。

## 实用句型 Useful Sentences

### 1. Wh-疑问句 (Wh- Questions)

- **What is this?** — 这是什么？
  - 回答：**It is a book.** 这是一本书。
- **Where is my shirt?** — 我的衬衫在哪里？
  - 回答：**It is on the bed.** 在床上。
- **When do you go to work?** — 你什么时候去上班？
  - 回答：**I go to work at eight.** 我八点去。
- **Who is that woman?** — 那位女士是谁？
  - 回答：**She is my mother.** 她是我妈妈。
- **Why do you like coffee?** — 你为什么喜欢咖啡？
  - 回答：**Because it is good.** 因为它好喝。
- **How do you go to school?** — 你怎么去上学？
  - 回答：**I go by bus.** 我坐公交车去。

### 2. Can / May 请求与回答

- **Can you help me?** — 你能帮我吗？
  - 肯定回答：**Yes, I can.** 是的，我能。
  - 否定回答：**Sorry, I can't.** 对不起，我不能。
- **Can I open the window?** — 我能打开窗户吗？
  - 回答：**Yes, please.** 好的，请打开。
- **May I come in?** — 我可以进来吗？
  - 回答：**Yes, please come in.** 请进。
- **May I have some water?** — 我可以喝点水吗？
  - 回答：**Sure, here you are.** 当然，给你。

### 3. 一般疑问句 (Yes/No Questions)

- **Do you like tea?** — 你喜欢茶吗？
  - 肯定：**Yes, I do.**
  - 否定：**No, I don't.**
- **Is she your sister?** — 她是你妹妹吗？
  - 肯定：**Yes, she is.**
  - 否定：**No, she isn't.**
- **Can you swim?** — 你会游泳吗？
  - 肯定：**Yes, I can.**
  - 否定：**No, I can't.**

## 语法要点 Grammar Point：疑问句结构

### Wh-疑问句结构

Wh-疑问词 + 助动词/be动词 + 主语 + 其他？

| 疑问词 | 含义 | 问什么 | 示例 |
|--------|------|--------|------|
| What | 什么 | 事物、信息 | What is your name? |
| Where | 哪里 | 地点 | Where do you live? |
| When | 什么时候 | 时间 | When does she come? |
| Who | 谁 | 人物 | Who is that man? |
| Why | 为什么 | 原因 | Why are you late? |
| How | 怎样 | 方式/程度 | How old are you? |

### Can / May 情态动词用法

情态动词 can / may 后面**必须跟动词原形**：

| 句子类型 | 结构 | 示例 |
|----------|------|------|
| 肯定句 | 主语 + can + 动词原形 | I can swim. |
| 否定句 | 主语 + can't + 动词原形 | He can't come. |
| 疑问句 | Can + 主语 + 动词原形? | Can you help me? |
| 请求句 | May + I + 动词原形? | May I sit here? |

### 如何回答 Yes/No 问句

回答一般疑问句时，用 **Yes/No + 简短回答**，不用只说 Yes/No：

| 问句 | 肯定回答 | 否定回答 |
|------|----------|----------|
| Do you like tea? | Yes, I **do**. | No, I **don't**. |
| Is she your friend? | Yes, she **is**. | No, she **isn't**. |
| Can you help? | Yes, I **can**. | No, I **can't**. |

## 对话练习 Conversation Practice

### 对话1：问路 Asking for Help

- **A**: Excuse me, can you help me?
- **B**: Yes, of course. What do you need?
- **A**: Where is the hospital?
- **B**: It is on Green Street. Go straight and turn left.
- **A**: Thank you for your help!
- **B**: You're welcome.

### 对话2：请求许可 Asking for Permission

- **A**: May I come in?
- **B**: Yes, please come in.
- **A**: Can I open the window? It is hot in here.
- **B**: Sure. Please open it.
- **A**: And can I have some water?
- **B**: Yes, here you are.

### 对话3：互相了解 Getting to Know Each Other

- **A**: Who is that man over there?
- **B**: He is my brother, Tom.
- **A**: What does he do?
- **B**: He is a student. He studies at university.
- **A**: How old is he?
- **B**: He is twenty years old.
- **A**: Why does he wear a blue shirt?
- **B**: Because he likes blue!

### 对话4：请求帮助 At a Restaurant

- **A**: Can I have the menu, please?
- **B**: Yes, here it is.
- **A**: What do you recommend?
- **B**: The fish is very good today.
- **A**: OK, I want the fish. And some water, please.
- **B**: Sure. Is that all?
- **A**: Yes, thank you.
