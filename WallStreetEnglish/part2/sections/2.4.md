### 词汇小贴士

- **星期记忆法**：星期一到星期日都以 -day 结尾，记住前两个 Monday（月亮日）和 Tuesday（战神日）就能推出其他。
- **月份缩写**：Jan. / Feb. / Mar. / Apr. / May / Jun. / Jul. / Aug. / Sep. / Oct. / Nov. / Dec.
- **天气问句**：What's the weather like?（天气怎么样？）是最常用的问法。

## 实用句型 Useful Sentences

### 1. 时间表达（What time is it?）

- **What time is it?** — 几点了？
  - 回答：**It is six o'clock.** 六点了。
- **It is half past seven.** — 七点半。
  - 用法：half past + 小时，表示"……点半"。
- **It is a quarter past eight.** — 八点一刻。
  - 用法：a quarter past = 15分过。
- **It is a quarter to nine.** — 八点三刻（九点差一刻）。
  - 用法：a quarter to = 差一刻到……点。

### 2. 日期表达（What day is it today?）

- **What day is it today?** — 今天星期几？
  - 回答：**Today is Monday.** 今天星期一。
- **What is the date today?** — 今天几号？
  - 回答：**It is March 15th.** 今天是3月15日。
- **My birthday is in July.** — 我的生日在七月。
  - 用法：月份前用 in (in January / in February)。
- **I go to work on Monday.** — 我星期一去上班。
  - 用法：星期前用 on (on Monday / on Tuesday)。

### 3. 天气表达（It is...today.）

- **It is sunny today.** — 今天天气晴朗。
  - 用法：It is + 天气形容词，描述天气。
- **It is cold and rainy.** — 天气又冷又下雨。
  - 用法：用 and 连接两个天气描述。
- **What is the weather like in spring?** — 春天天气怎么样？
  - 回答：**It is warm and sunny.** 温暖晴朗。
- **It is very hot in summer.** — 夏天很热。
  - 用法：very 放在形容词前加强语气。

## 语法要点 Grammar Point：介词 in / on / at

时间介词是A1级别的重要语法点：

| 介词 | 用法 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| **at** | 具体时间点 | at six o'clock, at 7:30 |
| **on** | 星期 / 具体日期 | on Monday, on January 1st |
| **in** | 月份 / 季节 / 年 | in March, in summer, in 2024 |

### 时间表达完整表格

| 表达 | 英文 | 介词 |
|------|------|------|
| 6点 | six o'clock | at |
| 7点半 | half past seven | at |
| 星期一 | Monday | on |
| 星期一早上 | Monday morning | on |
| 一月 | January | in |
| 春天 | spring | in |
| 2024年 | 2024 (twenty twenty-four) | in |

## 对话练习 Conversation Practice

### 对话1：谈论时间 Talking About Time

- **A**: Excuse me, what time is it?
- **B**: It is half past eight.
- **A**: Oh! I go to work at nine o'clock.
- **B**: You have time. Don't worry.
- **A**: What time do you go to work?
- **B**: I go to work at eight o'clock.

### 对话2：谈论天气 Talking About Weather

- **A**: What is the weather like today?
- **B**: It is sunny and warm.
- **A**: That's nice! I like sunny days.
- **B**: Do you like summer?
- **A**: Yes, I love summer. But summer in my city is very hot.
- **B**: I like spring. Spring is warm and beautiful.

### 对话3：谈论日期和计划 Plans and Dates

- **A**: What day is it today?
- **B**: Today is Monday.
- **A**: What is the date?
- **B**: It is March 20th.
- **A**: Oh, my birthday is in March!
- **B**: Really? When is your birthday?
- **A**: It is on March 25th.
- **B**: That is next Saturday!
