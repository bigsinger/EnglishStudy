# 2.3 家庭与朋友 (Family & Friends)

> 对应华尔街英语 Level 4 | CEFR A1

## 学习目标 Learning Objectives

- ✅ 掌握15个家庭成员和描述人物的词汇
- ✅ 学会用"He/She is..."描述人的特征
- ✅ 学会用"I have a..."介绍家人和朋友
- ✅ 学会用形容词描述人的外貌和年龄

### 词汇小贴士

- **家庭成员记忆法**：记住核心词根 mother/father，然后组合记忆：grandmother（祖母）= grand + mother，grandfather（祖父）= grand + father。
- **beautiful vs handsome**：beautiful 通常形容女性或物，handsome 通常形容男性。
- **young vs old**：这两个词可以形容人（年轻/年长），也可以形容物（新/旧）。This is an old house. 这是一栋旧房子。

## 实用句型 Useful Sentences

### 1. He/She is...（他/她是……的）

- **She is my mother.** — 她是我的妈妈。
  - 用法：is 连接主语和描述内容。
- **He is tall and handsome.** — 他又高又英俊。
  - 用法：多个形容词用 and 连接。
- **My sister is young and beautiful.** — 我姐姐又年轻又美丽。
  - 用法：形容词放在 be 动词后面。

### 2. I have a...（我有一个……）

- **I have a brother.** — 我有一个兄弟。
  - 用法：介绍家庭成员。可数名词单数前加 a/an。
- **I have two sisters.** — 我有两个姐妹。
  - 用法：复数直接用数字 + 复数名词。
- **I have a good friend.** — 我有一个好朋友。
  - 用法：形容词 good 放在名词 friend 前面。

### 3. 更多描述句型

- **My father is old but my mother is young.** — 我父亲年纪大，但我母亲年轻。
  - 用法：but 表示转折，连接两个对比的句子。
- **Her husband is a doctor.** — 她的丈夫是医生。
  - 用法：介绍某人的职业，用 a/an + 职业名词。
- **This is a photo of my family.** — 这是我家的照片。
  - 用法：a photo of... 表示"……的照片"。

## 语法要点 Grammar Point：be动词的用法

be动词（am/is/are）在描述人物时非常重要。A1级别要掌握以下用法：

### be动词搭配

| 主语 | be动词 | 缩写 | 示例 |
|------|--------|------|------|
| I | am | I'm | I **am** tall. |
| He/She/It | is | He's / She's / It's | She **is** beautiful. |
| You/We/They | are | You're / We're / They're | They **are** young. |

### 描述人物的形容词顺序

英语中多个形容词修饰名词时，一般顺序是：**年龄 → 外形 → 颜色 → 名词**。A1级别只需记住简单搭配：

- a **young beautiful** woman（一位年轻美丽的女士）
- an **old tall** man（一位年长的高个子男人）

### 问句形式

- **Is he your brother?** — 他是你兄弟吗？
  - 回答：Yes, he is. / No, he isn't.
- **How old is your mother?** — 你妈妈多大年纪？
  - 回答：She is 45 years old.

## 对话练习 Conversation Practice

### 对话1：介绍家人 Introducing Family

**A**: This is my family photo.
**B**: Oh, nice! Who is this?
**A**: This is my mother. She is a teacher.
**B**: Is this your father?
**A**: Yes, he is. He is tall and handsome.
**B**: Do you have a sister?
**A**: Yes, I have one sister. She is young and beautiful.

### 对话2：描述朋友 Describing a Friend

**A**: Do you have a good friend?
**B**: Yes, I have a friend. His name is Li Ming.
**A**: What does he look like?
**B**: He is tall and young. He has short hair.
**A**: Is he handsome?
**B**: Yes, he is very handsome!

### 对话3：家人的职业 Family Jobs

**A**: What does your father do?
**B**: My father is a doctor. He works in a hospital.
**A**: And your mother?
**B**: My mother is a teacher. She works in a school.
**A**: Do you have a brother or sister?
**B**: I have a brother. He is young. He is a student.

## 自测练习 Self-Test

**1. 用 is 或 are 填空。**
- My mother ____ beautiful.
- They ____ my friends.
- I ____ tall.
- My brother and sister ____ young.

**2. 翻译句子。**
她有一个儿子和一个女儿。
→ ________________________________

**3. 选择正确的单词。**
She is ____ (beautiful / handsome).
He is a ____ (beautiful / handsome) man.

**4. 连词成句。**
sister / my / is / beautiful / young / and
→ ________________________________

**5. 根据提示回答问题。**
Do you have a brother? (No)
→ ________________________________**答案**：
1. is; are; am; are
2. She has a son and a daughter.
3. beautiful; handsome
4. My sister is young and beautiful.
5. No, I don't have a brother. / No, I don't.
