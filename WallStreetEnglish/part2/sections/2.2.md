# 2.2 现在时态与简单句 (Present Tense & Simple Sentences)

> 对应华尔街英语 Level 3-4 | CEFR A1

## 学习目标 Learning Objectives

- ✅ 掌握一般现在时肯定句

### 词汇小贴士

- like 和 love 的区别：like 表示"喜欢"，程度较轻；love 表示"爱/非常喜欢"，程度更深。例如：I like tea but I love coffee.（我喜欢茶，但我热爱咖啡。）
- know 和 understand 的区别：know 是"知道事实"，understand 是"理解含义"。I know the word, but I don't understand the sentence.

## 实用句型 Useful Sentences

### 1. 肯定句：主语 + 动词

- **I eat bread for breakfast.** — 我早餐吃面包。
  - 用法：陈述每天做的事，动词用原形。
- **She drinks milk every day.** — 她每天喝牛奶。
  - 用法：第三人称单数（he/she/it）动词要加 -s 或 -es。
- **He works in an office.** — 他在办公室工作。
  - 用法：work → works，一般加 -s。

### 2. 否定句：主语 + don't/doesn't + 动词原形

- **I don't drink coffee.** — 我不喝咖啡。
  - 用法：I/You/We/They 用 don't (do not)。
- **He doesn't eat meat.** — 他不吃肉。
  - 用法：He/She/It 用 doesn't (does not)，后面动词恢复原形。
- **We don't live in that house.** — 我们不住在那栋房子里。
  - 用法：don't = do not，口语中常用缩写形式。

### 3. 疑问句：Do/Does + 主语 + 动词原形？

- **Do you like tea?** — 你喜欢茶吗？
  - 用法：Do + I/You/We/They + 动词原形。
- **Does she read books?** — 她看书吗？
  - 用法：Does + He/She/It + 动词原形（注意动词不加 -s）。
- **Where do you live?** — 你住在哪里？
  - 用法：疑问词 + do/does + 主语 + 动词原形。

## 语法要点 Grammar Point：一般现在时

一般现在时（Simple Present Tense）用来表示**经常发生的动作**或**客观事实**。

### be动词 vs 普通动词

英语有两类动词，变化规则不同：

| 类型 | 肯定句 | 否定句 | 疑问句 |
|------|--------|--------|--------|
| **be动词** (am/is/are) | I **am** a student. | I **am not** a student. | **Are** you a student? |
| **普通动词** (eat, like...) | I **eat** rice. | I **don't eat** rice. | **Do** you eat rice? |

### 第三人称单数动词变化规则

| 规则 | 举例 |
|------|------|
| 大多数动词 + s | eat → eats, like → likes, live → lives |
| 以 s/x/ch/sh/o 结尾 + es | go → goes, watch → watches |
| 辅音+y 变 i + es | study → studies |
| have 特殊变化 | have → has |

### 常见时间标志词

这些词提示我们要用一般现在时：
- every day / every week / every morning
- always（总是）
- usually（通常）
- sometimes（有时候）
- never（从不）

## 对话练习 Conversation Practice

### 对话1：日常习惯 Daily Habits

**A**: What do you eat for breakfast?
**B**: I eat bread and drink milk. What about you?
**A**: I eat rice and drink tea. I don't like milk.
**B**: Does your brother drink milk?
**A**: Yes, he drinks milk every morning.

### 对话2：工作和居住 Work and Live

**A**: Where do you live?
**B**: I live in Shanghai.
**A**: Do you work here?
**B**: Yes, I work in a school. I like my job.
**A**: Does your wife work too?
**B**: Yes, she works in a hospital. She loves her job.

### 对话3：爱好与偏好 Hobbies

**A**: Do you like reading?
**B**: Yes, I love reading. I read books every day.
**A**: Do you write books?
**B**: No, I don't write books. I only read them.
**A**: I understand. I like reading too.

## 自测练习 Self-Test

**1. 用动词的正确形式填空。**
- She ____ (drink) tea every morning.
- They ____ (go) to school by bus.
- He ____ (not / like) coffee.
- ____ you ____ (understand) the question?

**2. 改写成否定句。**
I eat meat.
→ ________________________________

**3. 改写成疑问句。**
She lives in Beijing.
→ ________________________________

**4. 翻译句子。**
我每天读一本书。
→ ________________________________

**5. 判断对错，并改正。**
He don't like tea.
→ ________________________________**答案**：
1. drinks; go; doesn't like; Do...understand
2. I don't eat meat.
3. Does she live in Beijing?（注意：疑问句动词用原形 live，不用 lives）
4. I read a book every day.
5. ❌ He don't like tea. → He **doesn't** like tea.
