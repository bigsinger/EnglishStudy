# 4.1 工作与职场沟通 (Workplace Communication)

## 学习目标 Learning Objectives

- 掌握15个与工作和职场相关的B1级核心词汇及其用法
- 学会用英语介绍自己的工作职责
- 掌握职场中的礼貌请求和沟通技巧
- 能够用英语参与基本的会议讨论和工作汇报

## 实用句型 Useful Sentences

1. **I work as a marketing manager at a technology company.**
   我在一家科技公司担任市场经理。

2. **I'm responsible for managing the sales team and developing new business opportunities.**
   我负责管理销售团队和开拓新的商业机会。

3. **Could you please send me the report before the meeting?**
   你能在会议之前把报告发给我吗？

4. **I have experience in project management and team leadership.**
   我在项目管理和团队领导方面有经验。

5. **We need to finish this task before the deadline.**
   我们需要在截止日期之前完成这项任务。

6. **I'm working on a new project with my colleagues from the design team.**
   我正在和设计部门的同事们一起做一个新项目。

7. **Would you like to discuss this in the meeting tomorrow?**
   你想在明天的会议上讨论这个吗？

8. **I'm looking for new career opportunities in international business.**
   我正在寻找国际商务方面的新职业机会。

## 语法要点 Grammar Point

### 现在完成时描述工作经验 (Present Perfect for Work Experience)

在职场英语中，我们常用现在完成时来描述工作经验和成就。它的结构是：**have/has + 过去分词**。

**用法一：描述工作经验**
- I **have worked** in this company for three years.
  我在这家公司工作了三年。
- She **has managed** several international projects.
  她管理过几个国际项目。
- They **have gained** a lot of experience in sales.
  他们在销售方面积累了很多经验。

**用法二：描述近期完成的成就**
- We **have finished** the quarterly report.
  我们已经完成了季度报告。
- He **has received** a promotion to senior manager.
  他已经晋升为高级经理。

**用法三：与 ever/never 连用**
- Have you **ever attended** an international conference?
  你参加过国际会议吗？
- I have **never missed** a deadline.
  我从未错过截止日期。

### 情态动词用于礼貌请求 (Modal Verbs for Polite Requests)

- **Could** you please send me the documents? (最礼貌)
- **Would** you mind checking this report? (同样礼貌)
- **Can** you help me with this task? (较非正式)

## 对话练习 Conversation Practice

**对话：第一次工作会议 (First Team Meeting)**

- **A:** Hi everyone, I'm David. I work as a new project manager in the marketing department. I'm responsible for the new advertising campaign.

- **B:** Welcome, David! I'm Sarah from the design team. This is my colleague, Mike. We'll be working with you on this project.

- **A:** Great! Could you please introduce me to the rest of the team?

- **B:** Of course. There are eight people in our team. We have a meeting every Tuesday to discuss progress.

- **A:** That sounds good. By the way, what's the deadline for this project?

- **B:** The deadline is next month, on the 15th. We have several tasks to complete before then.

- **A:** I see. I have experience in advertising campaigns, so I believe we can work together efficiently. Let's arrange a meeting tomorrow to go over the details.

- **B:** Perfect. I'll send you an invitation for the meeting.
