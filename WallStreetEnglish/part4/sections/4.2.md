# 4.2 社交与交友 (Socializing and Making Friends)

## 学习目标 Learning Objectives

- 掌握15个与社交活动和交友相关的B1级核心词汇
- 学会用英语发出和接受邀请
- 能够用英语提出活动建议并讨论共同兴趣
- 培养在社交场合中自然交流的能力

## 实用句型 Useful Sentences

1. **Would you like to join us for dinner this Saturday?**
   你愿意这周六和我们一起吃晚饭吗？

2. **How about going to the concert together this weekend?**
   这个周末一起去听音乐会怎么样？

3. **Let's meet at the restaurant at seven o'clock.**
   我们七点在餐厅见面吧。

4. **I really enjoy spending time with you.**
   我真的很喜欢和你在一起。

5. **We have a lot in common — we both love traveling and reading.**
   我们有很多共同点——我们都喜欢旅行和阅读。

6. **Thank you for inviting me to the party. It was great fun!**
   谢谢你邀请我参加聚会，太好玩了！

7. **What do you like to do in your free time?**
   你空闲时间喜欢做什么？

8. **It's my pleasure to introduce you to my friends.**
   我很荣幸把你介绍给我的朋友们。

## 语法要点 Grammar Point

### 提出建议的三种方式 (Three Ways to Make Suggestions)

**1. Would you like to...? (礼貌邀请，询问意愿)**
- **Would you like to** go to the cinema with me tonight?
  你今晚想和我一起去看电影吗？
- **Would you like to** join our book club?
  你想加入我们的读书俱乐部吗？

*回答：*
- Yes, I'd love to! (是的，我很乐意！)
- That sounds great, but I'm busy this weekend. (听起来不错，但我这周末很忙。)

**2. How about...? (非正式建议，后接名词或动名词)**
- **How about** watching a movie tonight?
  今晚看电影怎么样？
- **How about** a cup of coffee after work?
  下班后喝杯咖啡怎么样？

*回答：*
- Good idea! (好主意！)
- Sounds good! (听起来不错！)

**3. Let's... (直接建议，号召行动)**
- **Let's** go to the new restaurant this Friday.
  我们周五去那家新餐厅吧。
- **Let's** invite Tom to the party too.
  我们也邀请汤姆来聚会吧。

*回答：*
- OK, let's do that! (好，就这么办！)
- Why not? (为什么不呢？)

## 对话练习 Conversation Practice

**对话：邀请新朋友 (Inviting a New Friend)**

A: Hey, Lisa! It's great to see you again. How have you been?

B: Hi, David! I've been great, thanks. I just got back from a trip to Japan.

A: Wow, that sounds amazing! I've always wanted to travel there. How was it?

B: It was wonderful! The culture, the food — everything was fantastic.

A: You know, we should hang out more often. We have a lot in common — we both love traveling and trying new food.

B: Absolutely! Would you like to grab dinner this weekend? There's a new Japanese restaurant downtown.

A: I'd love to! How about Saturday evening?

B: Perfect. Let's meet at six o'clock in front of the restaurant.

A: Great! I can't wait. Is there anything I should bring?

B: Just yourself! It's my pleasure to have dinner with a fellow travel lover.
