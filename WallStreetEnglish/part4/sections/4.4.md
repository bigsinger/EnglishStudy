# 4.4 未来计划与打算 (Future Plans and Intentions)

## 学习目标 Learning Objectives

- 掌握15个与未来计划和打算相关的B1级核心词汇
- 学会用 be going to 和 will 表达未来计划
- 掌握现在进行时表示将来安排
- 能够用英语谈论短期和长期的目标与梦想

## 实用句型 Useful Sentences

1. **I'm going to look for a new job after the New Year.**
   我打算新年过后找一份新工作。

2. **I will call you as soon as I arrive at the airport.**
   我一到机场就给你打电话。

3. **We are having a team dinner tomorrow evening.**
   我们明天晚上有团队晚餐。（已安排）

4. **I hope to get a promotion within the next six months.**
   我希望在接下来的六个月内得到晋升。

5. **My goal is to save enough money to buy a house.**
   我的目标是存够钱买房子。

6. **I'm planning to take a language course next semester.**
   我打算下学期报一个语言课程。

7. **She dreams of starting her own business someday.**
   她梦想有朝一日创办自己的公司。

8. **I promise I won't be late for the meeting again.**
   我保证下次开会不再迟到了。

## 语法要点 Grammar Point

### 三种表达未来的方式 (Three Ways to Talk about the Future)

**1. be going to + 动词原形 — 表示已决定好的计划或有迹象表明即将发生**

- I **am going to** start a new project next month.
  我下个月要开始一个新项目。（已计划好）
- Look at those dark clouds! It **is going to** rain.
  看那些乌云！要下雨了。（有迹象）

*肯定句：* 主语 + am/is/are + going to + 动词原形
*否定句：* 主语 + am/is/are + not + going to + 动词原形
*疑问句：* Am/Is/Are + 主语 + going to + 动词原形?

**2. will + 动词原形 — 表示临时决定、承诺、预测或意愿**

- Don't worry, I **will** help you with the task.
  别担心，我会帮你完成任务的。（承诺）
- The phone is ringing. I **will** answer it.
  电话在响。我去接。（临时决定）
- I think she **will** be a great manager.
  我认为她会是一位出色的经理。（预测）

*肯定句：* 主语 + will + 动词原形
*否定句：* 主语 + will not (won't) + 动词原形
*疑问句：* Will + 主语 + 动词原形?

**3. 现在进行时 (am/is/are + 动词ing) — 表示已安排好的将来事件（通常是个人安排）**

- I **am meeting** my friend for lunch tomorrow.
  我明天和朋友约了吃午饭。（已安排）
- She **is flying** to Shanghai next Monday.
  她下周一要飞往上海。（已预订）
- We **are having** a team building event this weekend.
  我们这个周末有团建活动。（已安排）

### 三种将来表达方式的对比

| 表达方式 | 用法 | 例子 |
|---------|------|------|
| be going to | 已决定的计划或预测 | I'm going to study abroad. |
| will | 临时决定、承诺、预测 | I'll help you with that. |
| 现在进行时 | 已安排好的事件 | I'm having dinner with Tom tonight. |

## 对话练习 Conversation Practice

**对话：谈论未来计划 (Talking about Future Plans)**

- **A:** So, do you have any plans for the future, Mark?

- **B:** Yes, actually. I'm going to apply for a master's degree in business administration next year.

- **A:** That sounds great! Which university are you planning to apply to?

- **B:** I'm hoping to get into London Business School. But of course, I'll need to prepare for the entrance exam first.

- **A:** I'm sure you will succeed. When are you going to start preparing?

- **B:** I'm starting a preparation course next month. I've already arranged everything.

- **A:** That's wonderful. And what about your current job? Are you going to quit?

- **B:** No, I'm going to work part-time while studying. I have no intention of giving up my job completely.

- **A:** That's a good plan. I hope everything works out for you!

- **B:** Thanks! And what about you? Any big plans?

- **A:** Well, I'm moving to a new apartment next week. After that, I will probably focus on my career goals for a while.

- **B:** Sounds like we both have an exciting year ahead!
