# 3.2 旅行与问路 (Travel & Directions)

> 对应华尔街英语 Level 7 | CEFR A2

## 学习目标 Learning Objectives

- 学会在旅行场景中询问方向和描述路线
- 掌握各种交通工具的表达方式
- 能够在机场、车站、酒店等场所进行基本沟通
- 熟练运用方位词和方向指令

## 实用句型 Useful Sentences

### 问路场景

1. **Excuse me, where is the...?** — 请问……在哪里？
   - 最常用的问路句型，注意以 Excuse me 开头表示礼貌。
   - 例如：Excuse me, where is the nearest subway station?

2. **How do I get to...?** — 我该怎么去……？
   - 询问路线时使用，后面可以接地点名词。
   - 例如：How do I get to the city center?

3. **Go straight / Turn left / Turn right** — 直走 / 左转 / 右转
   - 方向指令的核心短语，常和 on your left/right 搭配。
   - 例如：Go straight, then turn left at the traffic lights.

4. **Is it far from here?** — 离这里远吗？
   - 确认距离。回答可能是：It's about 10 minutes on foot.（步行大约10分钟。）

5. **How long does it take?** — 需要多长时间？
   - 询问旅途所需时间。例如：How long does it take by bus?

### 交通场景

6. **Which platform does the train leave from?** — 火车从哪个站台出发？
   - 在火车站询问站台信息。

7. **How much is a ticket to...?** — 到……的票多少钱？
   - 购买车票或机票时询问价格。单程：one-way / 往返：round-trip

8. **Where can I catch a taxi?** — 我在哪里可以打到出租车？
   - 寻找出租车停靠点。

9. **Is there a bus that goes to...?** — 有去……的公交车吗？
   - 询问公交路线。

10. **I'd like to check in, please.** — 我想办理入住/值机。
    - 酒店前台入住或机场值机柜台通用。

## 语法要点 Grammar Point

### 祈使句 (Imperative Sentences)

问路和指引方向时，大量使用**祈使句**——即没有主语的命令或建议句式。

**结构：动词原形 + 其他成分**

| 用途 | 例句 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 方向指令 | **Go** straight. | 直走 |
| 方向指令 | **Turn** left at the corner. | 在拐角左转 |
| 建议 | **Take** the number 5 bus. | 坐5路公交车 |
| 否定指令 | **Don't** turn right. | 不要右转 |

**与礼貌用语结合：**

为了更礼貌，可以在祈使句前或后加上 please：

- **Please go** straight.（请直走。）
- **Take** a seat, **please.**（请坐。）

**常用介词搭配：**

| 介词 | 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **on** | 在……上/边 | The hotel is **on** your left. |
| **at** | 在……点/交叉口 | Turn **at** the traffic lights. |
| **next to** | 在……旁边 | The bank is **next to** the supermarket. |
| **between** | 在……之间 | The restaurant is **between** the park and the library. |
| **across from** | 在……对面 | The station is **across from** the mall. |

## 对话练习 Conversation Practice

### 对话 1：问路去博物馆

**A:** Excuse me, I'm a little lost. Can you help me?
**B:** Sure! Where do you want to go?
**A:** How do I get to the National Museum?
**B:** It's not far from here. Go straight for two blocks.
**A:** Two blocks, okay.
**B:** Then turn left at the traffic lights. You'll see a big park on your right.
**A:** Left at the traffic lights. Got it.
**B:** Walk through the park, and the museum is on the other side. You can't miss it!
**A:** How long does it take?
**B:** About 15 minutes on foot.
**A:** Great, thank you so much!
**B:** You're welcome. Have a nice day!

### 对话 2：在火车站

**A:** Good morning. I'd like a ticket to Oxford, please.
**B:** One-way or round-trip?
**A:** Round-trip, please. I'm coming back today.
**B:** That's £35.60. Would you like a seat reservation?
**A:** No, thanks. How long is the journey?
**B:** About one hour. The train leaves from Platform 3.
**A:** Which platform?
**B:** Platform 3. It departs at 10:15.
**A:** Is there a bus from the station to the city center?
**B:** Yes, the number 2 bus goes directly there.
**A:** Perfect. Here's my card.
**B:** Thank you. Here's your ticket and receipt.

### 对话 3：酒店入住

**A:** Good evening. I'd like to check in, please. I have a reservation.
**B:** What's your name, please?
**A:** David Smith.
**B:** Let me check... Yes, Mr. Smith. A single room for three nights.
**A:** That's right.
**B:** Could I see your passport, please?
**A:** Sure, here you go.
**B:** Thank you. Your room is on the fifth floor, room 512. Here's your key card.
**A:** Is there a restaurant in the hotel?
**B:** Yes, on the first floor. Breakfast is from 7 to 10.
**A:** Great. And where can I park my car?
**B:** There's an underground parking lot. Take the elevator to B1.
**A:** Thank you very much!
**B:** You're welcome. Enjoy your stay!

## 自测练习 Self-Test

**1. 如果你想问去火车站的路，最合适的表达是：**
A. Where are you going?
B. How do I get to the train station?
C. What is the train station?

**2. 完成方向指令：**
Go ______ for two blocks, then ______ left at the bookstore.

**3. 选择正确的介词：**
The pharmacy is ______ the bank and the post office.
A. next to
B. between
C. across from

**4. 匹配问句和回答：**

| 问句 | 回答 |
|------|------|
| ① How long does it take? | a. About £45 one-way. |
| ② How much is a ticket? | b. About 20 minutes by taxi. |
| ③ Which platform? | c. Platform 6. |

**5. 翻译成英语：**
"请直走，在第二个路口右转。邮局就在你的左边。"**答案：**
1. B — 问路的标准表达
2. straight; turn — 直走两个街区，在书店左转
3. B — between A and B 表示在A和B之间
4. ①→b, ②→a, ③→c
5. "Please go straight, and turn right at the second crossing. The post office is on your left."
