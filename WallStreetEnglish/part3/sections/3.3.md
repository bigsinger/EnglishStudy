# 3.3 描述人物与事物 (Describing People & Things)

> 对应华尔街英语 Level 7 | CEFR A2

## 学习目标 Learning Objectives

- 学会用形容词描述人物的外貌和性格
- 掌握形容词比较级的构成和用法
- 能够描述物品的大小、新旧、速度等特征
- 能够比较两个或多个对象

## 实用句型 Useful Sentences

### 描述人物外貌

1. **He/She is tall and thin.** — 他/她个子高且瘦。
   - 使用多个形容词描述外貌：He is short with curly brown hair.

2. **He/She has long black hair.** — 他/她有长黑发。
   - 用 has/have 描述拥有某种特征：She has big blue eyes.

3. **He looks... / She looks...** — 他看起来…… / 她看起来……
   - 描述当下的状态或印象：You look tired. / She looks happy.

### 描述物品

4. **It's a big house with a small garden.** — 这是一栋有大花园的大房子。
   - 用 a/an + 形容词 + 名词结构：It's an old but beautiful painting.

5. **This one is cheaper than that one.** — 这个比那个便宜。
   - 比较级的基本用法。

### 比较两个事物

6. **A is bigger than B.** — A 比 B 大。
   - 比较级的基本结构：形容词比较级 + than

7. **A is as big as B.** — A 和 B 一样大。
   - 同级比较：as + 原级 + as

8. **A is not as expensive as B.** — A 没有 B 贵。
   - 否定同级比较。

## 语法要点 Grammar Point

### 形容词比较级 (Comparative Adjectives)

比较级用来比较**两个**人或事物。构成规则如下：

| 情况 | 规则 | 例词 | 比较级 |
|------|------|------|--------|
| 单音节词 | + -er | big, small, old | bigger, smaller, older |
| 以 e 结尾 | + -r | nice, late | nicer, later |
| 辅音+y 结尾 | y → ier | happy, angry, hungry | happier, angrier, hungrier |
| 重读闭音节 | 双写尾字母+er | big, hot, sad | bigger, hotter, sadder |
| 多音节词 (≥2) | more + 原级 | beautiful, expensive | more beautiful, more expensive |

**不规则比较级：**

| 原级 | 比较级 |
|------|--------|
| good / well | better |
| bad / badly | worse |
| far | farther / further |
| old | older / elder |

**例句对比：**

- My bag is **smaller** than yours. (我的包比你的小。)
- This book is **more interesting** than that one. (这本书比那本有趣。)
- She is **taller** than her brother. (她比她哥哥高。)
- This hotel is **better** and **cheaper** than the other one. (这家酒店比另一家更好更便宜。)
- Today is **worse** than yesterday. (今天比昨天更糟。)

**小心发音：** 比较级的 -er 发音为 /ər/。bigger → /ˈbɪɡər/，smaller → /ˈsmɔːlər/。

### 描述顺序 (Adjective Order)

当使用多个形容词修饰一个名词时，英语中有大致顺序：

**观点 → 大小 → 年龄 → 颜色 → 国籍 → 材质 → 名词**

- a **beautiful big old red Chinese wooden** table

但在 A2 阶段，只要记住"观点在前，事实在后"即可：
- a **nice small** house（观点→大小）
- a **big old** building（大小→年龄）

## 对话练习 Conversation Practice

### 对话 1：描述新朋友

- **A:** Did you meet Sarah at the party?
- **B:** Yes, I did. She's very nice!
- **A:** What does she look like?
- **B:** She's tall and thin. She has long curly hair and big brown eyes.
- **A:** Sounds beautiful!
- **B:** She is! And she's very friendly too. Her smile makes her look happy all the time.
- **A:** Is she taller than you?
- **B:** Yes, she's a little taller than me. Maybe 5'8"?
- **A:** I'd love to meet her. Is she as nice as you say?
- **B:** Even nicer! And she's funnier than most people I know.

### 对话 2：比较两个手机

- **A:** I'm looking for a new phone. Which one do you recommend?
- **B:** Well, this one is cheaper than that one.
- **A:** But is it as good?
- **B:** The camera is not as good. The more expensive one has a better camera.
- **A:** What about battery life?
- **B:** The cheaper one has a bigger battery. So it lasts longer.
- **A:** Really? Which one is faster?
- **B:** The expensive one is faster, but the difference is small.
- **A:** Hmm. I think the cheaper one is better for me. It's cheaper and the battery lasts longer.
- **B:** Good choice! This color is also more beautiful than the other one.

### 对话 3：比较两座城市

- **A:** Have you been to Shanghai?
- **B:** Yes, I lived there for two years.
- **A:** How is it compared to Beijing?
- **B:** Shanghai is bigger and more modern. But Beijing is older and more traditional.
- **A:** Which city is more expensive?
- **B:** Shanghai is more expensive. Rent is higher there.
- **A:** What about the food?
- **B:** I think Beijing has better street food. But Shanghai has more international restaurants.
- **A:** Which one do you like better?
- **B:** That's a hard question! I think Shanghai is more exciting, but Beijing has more history.

## 自测练习 Self-Test

**1. 写出下列形容词的比较级：**
a. big → ________
b. happy → ________
c. beautiful → ________
d. good → ________
e. fast → ________

**2. 选词填空（用比较级形式）：**
My new apartment is ________ (big) than my old one, but it's also ________ (expensive).

**3. 改正错误：**
"She is more taller than her sister."

**4. 翻译句子：**
这本书比那本书有趣。

**5. 选择正确的句子：**
A. This car is more fast than that one.
B. This car is faster than that one.
C. This car is fast than that one.**答案：**
1. a. bigger, b. happier, c. more beautiful, d. better, e. faster
2. bigger; more expensive
3. 应改为：She is taller than her sister.（taller 已经是比较级，不能用 more）
4. This book is more interesting than that one.
5. B — faster 是正确的单音节比较级形式
