# 3.1 购物与餐饮 (Shopping & Dining)

> 对应华尔街英语 Level 6 | CEFR A2

## 学习目标 Learning Objectives

- 学会在商店和餐厅中进行完整的购物和点餐对话
- 掌握询问价格、尺寸、颜色等常见问题的表达方式
- 能够用英语向服务员提出请求和要求
- 学会看懂菜单并完成点餐、结账的全流程

## 实用句型 Useful Sentences

### 购物场景

1. **Can I help you? / May I help you?** — 有什么可以帮您的吗？
   - 店员常用开场白，顾客可以回答 "Yes, please." 或 "No, thanks. I'm just looking."

2. **I'd like to buy...** — 我想买……
   - 礼貌地表达购买意向，例如：I'd like to buy a pair of shoes.

3. **How much is this? / How much are these?** — 这个多少钱？
   - 询问价格。单数用 is，复数用 are：How much are these apples?

4. **Can I try this on?** — 我可以试穿这个吗？
   - 试穿衣服或鞋子时使用。Where is the fitting room?（试衣间在哪里？）

5. **Do you have this in a smaller size?** — 这个有更小的尺码吗？
   - 需要换尺码时的标准问法。也可以替换颜色：Do you have this in red?

### 餐饮场景

6. **I'd like to order...** — 我想点……
   - 点餐时使用，例如：I'd like to order a steak, please.

7. **What do you recommend?** — 你有什么推荐吗？
   - 不确定吃什么时询问服务员。

8. **Could I have the bill, please?** — 请给我账单。
   - 餐后要求结账。也可以说：Check, please.（美式英语）

9. **Is service included?** — 包含服务费吗？
   - 确认账单是否已包含小费或服务费。

10. **Can I pay by card?** — 我可以用卡支付吗？
    - 询问支付方式。回答：Cash or card?（现金还是刷卡？）

## 语法要点 Grammar Point

### 可数名词与不可数名词 (Countable & Uncountable Nouns)

在购物和餐饮场景中，你需要区分**可数名词**和**不可数名词**，因为它们的用法不同。

| 类别 | 特点 | 例子 |
|------|------|------|
| **可数名词** | 有复数形式，可用 a/an | apple(s), shirt(s), shoe(s), dollar(s) |
| **不可数名词** | 无复数形式，不可用 a/an | money, water, rice, bread, information |

**表达数量的方式：**

- **How much + 不可数名词：** How much **rice** do you want?
- **How many + 可数名词复数：** How many **apples** do you want?
- **a lot of / some / any** 可用于两者：
  - I'd like **some** water. (不可数)
  - I'd like **some** cookies. (可数复数)
  - Do you have **any** milk? (不可数，疑问句)

### 名词单复数 Example

| 单数 | 复数 | 发音 |
|------|------|------|
| one dollar | two dollars | /z/ |
| one shirt | two shirts | /s/ |
| one size | two sizes | /ɪz/ |

## 对话练习 Conversation Practice

### 对话 1：在服装店

**A:** Good afternoon! Can I help you?
**B:** Yes, please. I'd like to buy a jacket.
**A:** Sure! What color are you looking for?
**B:** I'm not sure. Maybe black or dark blue.
**A:** We have both. What size do you wear?
**B:** I think I'm a medium. Can I try this black one on?
**A:** Of course! The fitting room is over there.
**B:** (after trying) I like it, but it's a little big. Do you have a small?
**A:** Let me check... Yes, here you go.
**B:** This one fits perfectly! How much is it?
**A:** It's $89.99.
**B:** That's a bit expensive. Is there a discount?
**A:** Sorry, this item is not on sale.
**B:** Okay, I'll take it. Can I pay by card?
**A:** Yes, we accept all cards. Please come to the register.

### 对话 2：在餐厅

**A:** Good evening! Table for two?
**B:** Yes, please. Could we have a table by the window?
**A:** Of course. Right this way. Here are the menus.
**B:** Thank you. (looking at menu) Everything looks good!
**A:** Are you ready to order?
**B:** I think so. I'd like the grilled chicken salad.
**A:** Great choice! And for you, sir?
**C:** I'll have the steak, medium rare, please.
**A:** Would you like anything to drink?
**B:** I'll have a glass of water, please.
**C:** A Coke for me, please.
**A:** Perfect. I'll be right back with your drinks.

*(after the meal)*

**B:** Excuse me, could I have the bill, please?
**A:** Sure. Here you go. That's $52.60 in total.
**B:** Can I pay by credit card?
**A:** Yes, we accept Visa and MasterCard.

## 自测练习 Self-Test

**1. 选择正确的表达：** 在商店里，你看到一件喜欢的T恤，想问价格，应该说：
A. How many is this T-shirt?
B. How much is this T-shirt?
C. What size is this T-shirt?

**2. 完成对话：**
A: Can I ______ you?
B: Yes, I'd like to buy a pair of shoes.

**3. 选词填空（price / menu / receipt）：**
The waiter gave us a ______ to look at the food options.

**4. 判断正误：** "I'd like some waters, please." 这句话对吗？如果不对，应该怎么说？

**5. 角色扮演排序：** 将以下句子按餐厅用餐的正确顺序排列：
a. Could I have the bill, please?
b. I'd like the pasta, please.
c. Can I see the menu?
d. The food was delicious!**答案：**
1. B — How much is this T-shirt?（询问价格）
2. help — Can I help you? 是店员标准问候语
3. menu — 服务员给了我们菜单看食物选择
4. 不对。Water 是不可数名词，应该说 "I'd like some water, please."
5. 正确顺序：c → b → d → a（先看菜单，再点餐，然后用餐，最后结账）
