# 3.5 爱好与休闲 (Hobbies & Leisure)

> 对应华尔街英语 Level 8-9 | CEFR A2

## 学习目标 Learning Objectives

- 学会用英语谈论自己的兴趣爱好和休闲活动
- 掌握表达喜欢和不喜欢的多种方式
- 能够邀请他人一起参加活动并做出回应
- 学会描述休闲时间的安排和计划

## 实用句型 Useful Sentences

### 表达喜欢

1. **I like / love / enjoy + V-ing / noun** — 我喜欢……
   - **like** 表示喜欢，**love** 表示非常喜欢，**enjoy** 强调享受过程。
   - 例如：I like reading books. / I love playing basketball. / I enjoy cooking.

2. **I'm interested in + V-ing / noun** — 我对……感兴趣
   - 较正式的表达：I'm interested in photography.

3. **I'm a big fan of...** — 我是……的忠实粉丝
   - 口语常用：I'm a big fan of rock music.

4. **My favorite... is...** — 我最喜欢的……是……
   - 例如：My favorite sport is swimming.

### 表达不喜欢

5. **I don't like / don't enjoy / hate + V-ing / noun** — 我不喜欢……
   - 程度从弱到强：don't like → don't really like → hate
   - 例如：I don't like getting up early. / I hate cold weather.

6. **I'm not interested in...** — 我对……不感兴趣
   - 礼貌地表示不感兴趣：I'm not interested in video games.

7. **It's not my thing.** — 这不是我的菜。
   - 非常口语化的表达：Running is not my thing. I prefer walking.

### 邀请和提议

8. **Do you want to...?** — 你想……吗？
   - 发出邀请：Do you want to play tennis this weekend?

9. **How about + V-ing...?** — ……怎么样？
   - 提出建议：How about going to the cinema tonight?

10. **Would you like to...?** — 你想要……吗？
    - 更礼貌的邀请：Would you like to join us for a game of football?

## 语法要点 Grammar Point

### 动词的 -ing 形式 (Gerunds)

在谈论爱好时，动词经常以 **-ing** 形式（动名词）出现，充当句子的主语或宾语。

#### 1. 动名词作主语

**Swimming** is good for your health.（游泳对健康有益。）
**Reading** makes me relax.（阅读让我放松。）

#### 2. 动名词作宾语（放在 like / love / enjoy / hate 后面）

- I **like playing** basketball.
- She **enjoys listening** to music.
- He **hates getting** up early.

#### 3. -ing 形式的构成

| 规则 | 动词 | -ing 形式 |
|------|------|-----------|
| 直接 +ing | play, read, sing | playing, reading, singing |
| 以 e 结尾，去 e +ing | dance, make, take | dancing, making, taking |
| 重读闭音节，双写 +ing | run, swim, begin | running, swimming, beginning |

#### 4. 常用搭配总结

| 表达 | 搭配 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| like | like + V-ing | I like **playing** football. |
| love | love + V-ing | She loves **dancing**. |
| enjoy | enjoy + V-ing | He enjoys **traveling**. |
| hate / don't like | hate + V-ing | I hate **waiting**. |
| be good at | be good at + V-ing | She is good at **singing**. |
| be interested in | be interested in + V-ing | He is interested in **learning** Chinese. |

## 对话练习 Conversation Practice

### 对话 1：谈论爱好

**A:** What do you like to do in your free time?
**B:** I really enjoy playing basketball. What about you?
**A:** I like reading and listening to music.
**B:** What kind of music do you like?
**A:** I love pop music. My favorite singer is Taylor Swift.
**B:** Nice! Do you like playing any instruments?
**A:** Yes, I play the guitar a little. It's my hobby.
**B:** That's cool! How long have you been playing?
**A:** For about two years. I'm not very good, but I enjoy it.
**B:** Do you want to play together sometime? I sing a little.
**A:** Really? That sounds fun! Let's do that!

### 对话 2：周末计划

**A:** Do you have any plans for the weekend?
**B:** Not really. I'll probably just relax at home.
**A:** How about going hiking on Saturday? The weather will be nice.
**B:** Hmm, I'm not sure. Hiking is not really my thing.
**A:** What do you usually do on weekends?
**B:** I like watching movies and playing video games.
**A:** Me too! I'm a big fan of action movies. What about you?
**B:** I prefer comedies. I watched a funny movie last weekend.
**A:** Oh, which one?
**B:** It's called "The Hangover." Have you seen it?
**A:** Yes! That movie is so funny! I loved it.
**B:** Do you want to watch a movie together this weekend?
**A:** Sure! How about Saturday evening?
**B:** Sounds good! Let's meet at 7 PM.

### 对话 3：运动爱好

**A:** Do you play any sports?
**B:** Yes, I play football with my friends every Sunday.
**A:** That's great! Are you on a team?
**B:** Yeah, we have a small team. We play in a local league.
**A:** How often do you practice?
**B:** We practice twice a week — on Tuesdays and Thursdays.
**A:** That's a lot! I don't have that much free time.
**B:** What sports do you like?
**A:** I enjoy swimming and running. Swimming is my favorite.
**B:** How often do you go swimming?
**A:** Three times a week. I go to the pool near my house.
**B:** That's good exercise. Do you swim for fun or for competition?
**A:** Just for fun and to stay healthy. I'm not fast enough for competition!
**B:** Maybe we can do some sports together sometime.
**A:** I'd love to! But I'm not good at football.
**B:** Don't worry! We can go swimming together instead.

## 自测练习 Self-Test

**1. 用 like / love / enjoy / hate 填空（注意动词形式）：**
a. I ________ (read) books in my free time.
b. She ________ (not like) playing video games.
c. They ________ (enjoy) traveling to new places.
d. He ________ (hate) getting up early on weekends.

**2. 将下列句子改成否定句：**
I like running. → I ________ ________ running.

**3. 选择正确的回答：**
A: Do you want to play tennis this weekend?
B: ________
A. Yes, I like.
B. Sure, I'd love to!
C. Yes, I want.

**4. 用 -ing 形式改写：**
I like to dance. → I like ________.

**5. 翻译成英语：**
a. 你喜欢在你的空闲时间做什么？
b. 我最喜欢的运动是游泳。
c. 我不喜欢跑步。我喜欢散步。**答案：**
1. a. like/love/enjoy reading, b. doesn't like, c. enjoy traveling, d. hates
2. I don't like running.
3. B — "Sure, I'd love to!" 是接受邀请的自然表达
4. I like dancing.
5. a. What do you like to do in your free time?
   b. My favorite sport is swimming.
   c. I don't like running. I like walking.
