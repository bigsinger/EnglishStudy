# 3.4 过去时态与经历 (Past Tense & Experiences)

> 对应华尔街英语 Level 8 | CEFR A2

## 学习目标 Learning Objectives

- 掌握一般过去时的肯定句、否定句和疑问句
- 区分规则动词和不规则动词的过去式
- 能够用英语描述过去发生的事情和经历
- 学会使用时间标记词（yesterday, last week, ago 等）

## 实用句型 Useful Sentences

### 描述过去的经历

1. **I went to the park yesterday.** — 我昨天去了公园。
   - 使用过去时间标记词。常用时间词：yesterday, last night, last week, ...ago, in 2020

2. **What did you do last weekend?** — 你上周末做了什么？
   - 一般过去时的疑问句结构：Did + 主语 + 动词原形？

3. **I didn't go to work yesterday.** — 我昨天没去上班。
   - 否定句：didn't + 动词原形

4. **Did you have a good time?** — 你玩得开心吗？
   - 询问体验和感受。回答：Yes, I did. / No, I didn't.

### 谈论旅行和体验

5. **Have you ever been to...?** — 你去过……吗？
   - 注意：虽然这是现在完成时，但在 A2 阶段作为固定短语学习。
   - 回答：Yes, I went there last year.

6. **What was the best part?** — 最精彩的部分是什么？
   - 询问过去的体验中印象最深的部分。

7. **I had a great time.** — 我玩得很开心。
   - 表达过去的愉快体验。

8. **It was amazing / terrible.** — 太棒了 / 太糟糕了。
   - 用 was/were 描述过去的状态或感受。

## 语法要点 Grammar Point

### 一般过去时 (Simple Past Tense)

一般过去时表示**过去某个时间发生并已经完成**的动作或状态。

#### 1. 规则动词的过去式

| 规则 | 原形 | 过去式 | 发音 |
|------|------|--------|------|
| 直接 +-ed | work / play | worked / played | /d/ 或 /t/ |
| 以 e 结尾 +-d | like / live | liked / lived | /d/ |
| 辅音+y → ied | study / try | studied / tried | /d/ |
| 重读闭音节双写+ed | stop / plan | stopped / planned | /d/ |

**发音规则：**
- -ed 在清辅音后读 /t/：**worked** /wɜːrkt/, **watched** /wɒtʃt/
- -ed 在浊辅音和元音后读 /d/：**played** /pleɪd/, **lived** /lɪvd/
- -ed 在 t/d 后读 /ɪd/：**started** /ˈstɑːrtɪd/, **visited** /ˈvɪzɪtɪd/

#### 2. 不规则动词

英语中有大量不规则动词，需要逐个记忆。以下是高频不规则动词表：

| 原形 | 过去式 | 例句 |
|------|--------|------|
| go | went | I **went** to the store. |
| have | had | She **had** a great idea. |
| do | did | He **did** his homework. |
| see | saw | We **saw** a movie. |
| eat | ate | They **ate** dinner at 7. |
| buy | bought | I **bought** a gift. |
| take | took | She **took** a photo. |
| make | made | He **made** a mistake. |
| speak | spoke | I **spoke** to the teacher. |
| say | said | She **said** hello. |

#### 3. 句子结构

| 类型 | 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 肯定句 | 主语 + 过去式 + 其他 | I **played** tennis yesterday. |
| 否定句 | 主语 + didn't + 动词原形 + 其他 | I **didn't play** tennis yesterday. |
| 一般疑问句 | Did + 主语 + 动词原形 + 其他？ | **Did** you **play** tennis yesterday? |
| 特殊疑问句 | 疑问词 + did + 主语 + 动词原形？ | What **did** you **play** yesterday? |

**注意：** 否定句和疑问句中，动词**必须**用原形，因为 did 已经承担了"过去"的含义。

## 对话练习 Conversation Practice

### 对话 1：聊聊上周末

- **A:** Hi, Tom! How was your weekend?
- **B:** It was great! I went to the beach with my family.
- **A:** Sounds fun! What did you do there?
- **B:** We swam in the sea and played beach volleyball. I also took a lot of photos.
- **A:** Did you eat seafood?
- **B:** Yes! We ate fresh fish and shrimp at a small restaurant by the sea.
- **A:** Yummy! What did you have for dessert?
- **B:** We had ice cream. It was delicious!
- **A:** Did you buy anything?
- **B:** I bought a souvenir for my sister.
- **A:** Sounds like you had a wonderful weekend!
- **B:** Yeah, I really did. What about you? What did you do?
- **A:** Nothing special. I stayed home and watched a movie.

### 对话 2：谈论旅行经历

- **A:** Did you go anywhere last summer?
- **B:** Yes, I visited Japan for two weeks.
- **A:** Wow! Where did you go in Japan?
- **B:** I went to Tokyo, Kyoto, and Osaka.
- **A:** Which city did you like the most?
- **B:** I liked Kyoto the most. It was more traditional and beautiful.
- **A:** Did you try the local food?
- **B:** Of course! I ate sushi, ramen, and takoyaki. Everything was amazing.
- **A:** How was the weather?
- **B:** It was hot and a little humid, but not too bad.
- **A:** Did you speak Japanese?
- **B:** I tried! I spoke a few words, but most people spoke English to me.
- **A:** When did you get back?
- **B:** I came back last week. I bought some souvenirs for my friends.
- **A:** I'd love to visit Japan one day!
- **B:** You should go! It was one of the best trips I've ever had.

### 对话 3：昨天的工作

- **A:** How was work yesterday?
- **B:** Terrible! I was so busy. I started at 8 AM and finished at 8 PM.
- **A:** That's a long day! Did you take a lunch break?
- **B:** I took a short break, but I didn't have real lunch. I just ate a sandwich at my desk.
- **A:** That's not healthy. What did you work on?
- **B:** I worked on a big project. I made about 50 phone calls and wrote many emails.
- **A:** Did you speak to your boss?
- **B:** Yes, we had a meeting in the afternoon. It lasted two hours.
- **A:** Wow, you worked really hard. Did you finish the project?
- **B:** Not yet. I still have some work to do today.
- **A:** Good luck! I hope today is better!

## 自测练习 Self-Test

**1. 写出下列动词的过去式：**
a. go → ________
b. eat → ________
c. study → ________
d. see → ________
e. buy → ________

**2. 改写成过去时：**
I go to school every day. → Yesterday, I ________ to school.

**3. 改错：**
"She didn't went to the party."

**4. 变成疑问句：**
You visited the museum. → ________ you ________ the museum?

**5. 用括号中动词的正确形式填空：**
Last night I ________ (watch) a movie. I ________ (not like) it, but my friend ________ (love) it.**答案：**
1. a. went, b. ate, c. studied, d. saw, e. bought
2. Yesterday, I went to school.
3. 应改为：She didn't go to the party.（didn't 后面用动词原形）
4. Did you visit the museum?
5. watched; didn't like; loved
