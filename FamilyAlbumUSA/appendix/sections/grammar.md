# 附录D. 语法快速参考 — Grammar Quick Reference

> 本附录汇总《Family Album USA》教材中出现的核心语法要点，采用表格+例句形式，便于快速查阅。## 一、Parts of Speech 词性

英语十大词类及其在句中的作用：

| 词性 | 英文 | 作用 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|------|
| 名词 | Noun | 表示人、事物、地点或概念 | **Richard** is a **photographer**. |
| 代词 | Pronoun | 代替名词 | **She** loves **her** job. |
| 动词 | Verb | 表示动作或状态 | I **work** in an office. |
| 形容词 | Adjective | 修饰名词 | It's a **beautiful** day. |
| 副词 | Adverb | 修饰动词、形容词或句子 | She speaks **quickly**. |
| 介词 | Preposition | 表示方向、时间、位置等 | The book is **on** the table. |
| 连词 | Conjunction | 连接词、短语或句子 | I like coffee **and** tea. |
| 冠词 | Article | 限定名词 | **A** dog, **an** apple, **the** sun |
| 感叹词 | Interjection | 表达情感 | **Oh!** That's wonderful. |

> **注意**：同一个词在不同语境中可以是不同词性。例如 *book* 既可以是名词（书），也可以是动词（预订）。## 二、Tenses 时态

### 1. 一般现在时 (Simple Present)

| 用法 | 说明 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 习惯/经常性动作 | 常与 always, usually, often, sometimes, never 连用 | I **get up** at 7 every morning. |
| 客观事实/真理 | 不受时间影响的事实 | The sun **rises** in the east. |
| 状态动词 | 表示感觉、情感、拥有等 | She **likes** classical music. |
| 第三人称单数 | 动词加 -s/-es | He **works** at a bank. / She **goes** to school by bus. |

> **否定句**：I **do not (don't)** like coffee. / He **does not (doesn't)** smoke.
> **疑问句**：**Do** you speak English? / **Does** she live here?

### 2. 现在进行时 (Present Continuous)

| 用法 | 说明 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 正在进行的动作 | now, at the moment, currently | Look! It **is raining**. |
| 当前阶段但未必此刻 | 表示一段时间内正在做的事 | I **am reading** a great book these days. |
| 近期计划/安排 | 常与表示将来的时间连用 | We **are leaving** tomorrow morning. |

> **结构**：am/is/are + doing
> **否定**：She **is not (isn't) working** today.
> **不可用于进行时**的动词（状态动词）：know, believe, want, need, like, belong, etc. ✗ I am knowing → ✓ I know

### 3. 一般过去时 (Simple Past)

| 用法 | 说明 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 过去完成的动作 | yesterday, last week, in 2020, ago | I **visited** New York **last year**. |
| 过去的状态 | 过去是怎样的 | She **was** very happy. |
| 过去习惯 | used to 或 would 也可表达 | I **walked** to school every day when I was a child. |

> **规则动词**：加 -ed (work → worked)
> **不规则动词**：需记忆（go → went, see → saw, take → took）
> **否定**：I **did not (didn't) go**.
> **疑问**：**Did** you see the movie?

### 4. 现在完成时 (Present Perfect)

| 用法 | 说明 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 过去发生但影响现在 | 强调结果而非时间 | I **have lost** my keys.（现在找不到） |
| 经历/经验 | ever, never, before | **Have** you ever **been** to China? |
| 从过去持续到现在 | since/for, 动作或状态延续 | She **has lived** here **for** ten years. |
| 刚刚完成 | just, already, yet | He **has just finished** his homework. |

> **结构**：have/has + 过去分词
> **have been vs. have gone**：
> - She **has been** to Paris.（去过，已回来）
> - She **has gone** to Paris.（去了，还没回来）

### 5. 一般将来时 (Simple Future)

| 表达方式 | 用法 | 例句 |
|---------|------|------|
| will + 动词原形 | 预测、即时决定、承诺、意愿 | I **will help** you with that. |
| be going to + 动词原形 | 已有计划/打算、有迹象预示 | It **is going to rain** — look at those clouds! |
| 现在进行时 | 已安排好的计划（尤其跟时间） | I **am meeting** my boss at 3 p.m. |
| 一般现在时 | 时刻表、固定安排 | The train **leaves** at 8 p.m. tonight. |

> **will 与 be going to 的区别**：
> - A: "The phone is ringing!" B: "I **'ll get** it."（即时决定）
> - "I **am going to buy** a new car."（提前计划）

### 6. 将来进行时 (Future Continuous)

| 用法 | 说明 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 将来某时正在做某事 | at 8 p.m. tomorrow, this time next week | This time next week, I **will be flying** to London. |
| 将来的日常安排之一 | 语气比一般将来更加自然 | I **will be working** tomorrow morning, so I can't go. |

> **结构**：will be + doing
> **否定**：I **won't be sleeping** at midnight — I'll be studying.

### 时态速查表

| 时态 | 结构 | 关键词 |
|------|------|-------|
| 一般现在 | do / does | always, usually, every day |
| 现在进行 | am/is/are + doing | now, at the moment, listen! |
| 一般过去 | did / was/were | yesterday, last..., ago |
| 现在完成 | have/has + done | already, just, ever, never, since, for |
| 一般将来 | will + do / be going to + do | tomorrow, next..., soon |
| 将来进行 | will be + doing | this time tomorrow, at 8 p.m. |## 三、Modal Verbs 情态动词

| 情态动词 | 含义 | 用法 | 例句 |
|---------|------|------|------|
| **can** | 能，可以 | 能力、许可、可能性 | I **can** swim. / **Can** I use your pen? |
| **may** | 可能，可以 | 许可、可能性（较正式） | **May** I come in? / It **may** rain. |
| **must** | 必须 | 强制义务、必要性 | You **must** wear a seatbelt. |
| **should** | 应该 | 建议、义务（弱于 must） | You **should** see a doctor. |
| **ought to** | 应该 | 同 should，更正式 | You **ought to** apologize to her. |
| **have to** | 不得不 | 客观需要，时态可变化 | I **have to** finish this report today. / I **had to** leave early yesterday. |

### 情态动词的时态替换

| 情态动词 | 过去式/替换形式 | 例句 |
|---------|---------------|------|
| can → | could / be able to | I **could swim** when I was 5. / I **was able to** finish on time. |
| may → | might / be allowed to | She **might** come. / I **was allowed to** stay up late. |
| must → | had to | I **had to** work late last night. |
| should → | should have + 过去分词（过去本应做却没做） | You **should have told** me earlier. |

> **must vs. have to**：must 表示主观义务（自己认为必须），have to 表示客观需要（外部要求）。
> 例：I **must** lose weight.（我自己决心减肥）/ I **have to** go to work.（我必须去上班）

### 情态动词的否定

| 情态动词 | 否定形式 | 缩写 | 含义 | 例句 |
|---------|---------|------|------|------|
| can | cannot | can't | 不能，不会 | I **can't** drive. |
| may | may not | — | 可能不 | She **may not** come. |
| must | must not | mustn't | 禁止，绝不能 | You **must not** smoke here. |
| should | should not | shouldn't | 不应该 | You **shouldn't** eat too much sugar. |
| have to | do not have to | don't have to | 不必（≠ mustn't） | You **don't have to** wait for me. |

> ⚠️ **注意区分**：**mustn't**（禁止）≠ **don't have to**（不必）
> - You **mustn't** tell anyone.（你不能告诉任何人——这是秘密）
> - You **don't have to** tell anyone.（你不需要告诉任何人——随你便）## 四、Passive Voice 被动语态

| 时态 | 主动语态 | 被动语态 |
|------|---------|---------|
| 一般现在 | People **speak** English. | English **is spoken** (by people). |
| 一般过去 | Someone **stole** my bag. | My bag **was stolen**. |
| 现在进行 | They **are building** a bridge. | A bridge **is being built**. |
| 现在完成 | She **has finished** the report. | The report **has been finished**. |
| 一般将来 | They **will announce** the results. | The results **will be announced**. |
| 情态动词 | You **must sign** this form. | This form **must be signed**. |

> **结构**：be (各种时态) + 过去分词 (+ by...)
> **何时使用被动语态**：
> - 动作执行者不明确或不重要：My bike **was stolen**.
> - 侧重承受者而非执行者：The President **was welcomed** by the crowd.
> - 正式文体或科技文章中常用

> **注意**：并非所有动词都有被动语态。不及物动词（如 happen, arrive, die）没有被动形式。## 五、Clauses 从句

### 1. 定语从句 (Relative Clauses)

修饰名词或代词的从句，相当于一个形容词。

| 关系词 | 指代 | 在从句中的作用 | 例句 |
|--------|------|---------------|------|
| **who** | 人 | 主语、宾语 | The man **who lives next door** is a doctor. |
| **whom** | 人 | 宾语（正式） | The woman **whom I met** is from Canada. |
| **that** | 人或物 | 主语、宾语 | This is the book **that I bought**. |
| **which** | 物/动物 | 主语、宾语 | The house **which is on the corner** is for sale. |
| **whose** | 人/物 | 所有格 | That's the student **whose father** is a pilot. |

> **限制性 vs. 非限制性定语从句**：
> - 限制性（无逗号）：My brother **who lives in New York** is a lawyer.（我有多个兄弟，特指纽约那个）
> - 非限制性（有逗号）：My brother, **who lives in New York**, is a lawyer.（只有一个兄弟，补充信息）
> - **注意**：that 只能用于限制性定语从句，非限制性必须用 which/who

### 2. 宾语从句 (Noun Clauses as Objects)

在句中充当宾语的从句。

| 引导词 | 用法 | 例句 |
|--------|------|------|
| **that** | 陈述句作宾语（常省略） | I think **(that)** she is right. / He said **(that)** he was tired. |
| **if / whether** | 一般疑问句转化 | I don't know **if** he will come. / She asked **whether** I liked it. |
| 疑问词 (what/where/when等) | 特殊疑问句转化 | Can you tell me **where** the station is? / I don't know **what** she said. |

> **宾语从句的语序**：即使是疑问含义，从句也要用**陈述句语序**
> - ✓ I don't know **where he lives**.
> - ✗ I don't know **where does he live**.

> **时态呼应**：若主句是过去时，从句也要用相应的过去时态
> - He said he **was** busy.（原句：I am busy.）
> - She asked if I **had seen** the movie.（原句：Have you seen the movie?）

### 3. 条件句 (Conditional Sentences)

| 类型 | 条件从句 (if) | 主句 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|-------------|------|------|------|
| **零条件** | 一般现在时 | 一般现在时 | 客观真理/习惯 | If you **heat** ice, it **melts**. |
| **第一类** (Type 1) | 一般现在时 | will + 动词原形 | 将来很可能发生 | If it **rains**, I **will stay** at home. |
| **第二类** (Type 2) | 一般过去时 | would + 动词原形 | 与现在事实相反/不太可能 | If I **had** a million dollars, I **would travel** around the world. |
| **第三类** (Type 3) | 过去完成时 | would have + 过去分词 | 与过去事实相反 | If I **had studied** harder, I **would have passed** the exam. |

> **unless = if...not**
> - I won't go **unless** you come with me. = I won't go **if** you **don't** come with me.

> **第一类与第二类对比**：
> - Type 1（可能实现）：If I **have** time, I **will call** you.
> - Type 2（不可能/假设）：If I **had** time, I **would call** you.（实际没时间）## 六、Comparative Structures 比较结构

### 1. 比较级 (Comparative)

| 形容词/副词类型 | 构成 | 例句 |
|----------------|------|------|
| 单音节 | -er | tall → **taller**, fast → **faster** |
| 以 y 结尾 | -ier | happy → **happier**, early → **earlier** |
| 多音节 | more + adj | beautiful → **more beautiful** |
| 不规则 | 特殊形式 | good/well → **better**, bad/badly → **worse**, far → **farther/further** |

> **句型**：A + be + 比较级 + than + B
> - She is **taller than** her sister.
> - This book is **more interesting than** that one.

### 2. 最高级 (Superlative)

| 类型 | 构成 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 单音节 | -est | tall → **the tallest** |
| 以 y 结尾 | -iest | happy → **the happiest** |
| 多音节 | the most + adj | beautiful → **the most beautiful** |
| 不规则 | 特殊形式 | good → **the best**, bad → **the worst** |

> **句型**：A + be + the + 最高级 + (in/of/among...)
> - She is **the best** student **in** the class.
> - This is **the most expensive** car **of** all.

### 3. as...as 结构

| 结构 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| as + adj/adv + as | 和……一样 | She is **as tall as** her mother. |
| not as/so + adj/adv + as | 不如…… | This movie is **not as good as** the book. |
| as many + 可数名词 + as | 和……一样多 | He has **as many books as** I do. |
| as much + 不可数名词 + as | 和……一样多 | I don't have **as much money as** him. |

### 4. 倍数表达

| 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| 倍数 + as + adj/adv + as | This room is **twice as large as** that one. |
| 倍数 + 比较级 + than | He earns **three times more than** I do. |
| 倍数 + the + 名词 + of | The lake is **four times the size of** the park. |

> 常用倍数词：twice (两倍), three times (三倍), four times (四倍), half as...as (一半)## 七、Other Grammar Points 其他语法要点

### 1. There be 句型

| 结构 | 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| There is + 单数/不可数名词 | 存在某人/某物 | **There is** a book on the desk. |
| There are + 复数名词 | 存在多个 | **There are** many students in the room. |
| There was / There were | 过去存在 | **There was** a problem yesterday. |
| There will be / There has been | 将来/现在完成 | **There will be** a meeting at 3 p.m. |

> **就近原则**：动词 be 与最近的名词保持数的一致
> - **There is** a pen and two books on the table.
> - **There are** two books and a pen on the table.

### 2. Imperative Sentences 祈使句

| 类型 | 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 肯定祈使 | 动词原形 | **Sit** down. **Open** the door, please. |
| 否定祈使 | Don't + 动词原形 | **Don't** touch that! **Don't** be late. |
| 以 let 开头 | Let + 宾语 + 动词原形 | **Let's** go! **Let me** help you. |

### 3. Exclamatory Sentences 感叹句

| 结构 | 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| What + (a/an) + 形容词 + 名词 + (主语+谓语) | 感叹名词 | **What a beautiful day** (it is)! / **What nice people** (they are)! |
| How + 形容词/副词 + 主语 + 谓语 | 感叹形容词/副词 | **How lovely** she is! / **How fast** he runs! |
| How + 形容词 + a/an + 名词 + 主语 + 谓语 | 感叹可数名词 | **How beautiful a day** it is! |

### 4. Infinitive 不定式 (to do)

| 功能 | 说明 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 作主语 | 动词不定式充当主语 | **To learn** English is important. (= It is important **to learn** English.) |
| 作宾语 | 跟在某些动词后 | I want **to go** home. / She decided **to stay**. |
| 作定语 | 修饰名词 | I have a lot of work **to do**. |
| 作状语 | 表示目的 | He came here **to see** you. |
| 作宾语补足语 | 补充说明宾语 | I asked him **to help** me. / She told me **to wait**. |

> **不带 to 的不定式**（省略 to）：
> - 情态动词后：I can **swim**. / You must **go**.
> - 使役动词后 (let, make, have)：Let me **help** you. / It made me **cry**.
> - 感官动词后 (see, hear, watch, feel)：I saw him **leave**.

### 5. Gerund 动名词 (doing)

| 功能 | 说明 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 作主语 | 较不定式更常用 | **Swimming** is good exercise. |
| 作宾语 | 跟某些动词后 | I enjoy **reading**. / He finished **writing** the letter. |
| 作介词宾语 | 介词后必须用动名词 | Thank you for **coming**. / He's interested in **learning** Chinese. |
| 作定语 | 说明用途 | This is a **swimming** pool.（游泳池） |

> **后接动名词的常见动词**：enjoy, finish, avoid, mind, suggest, practice, consider, keep, admit, deny, miss, can't help
> **动名词 vs. 不定式**（意义不同）：
> - I stopped **smoking**.（我戒烟了——不再抽烟）
> - I stopped **to smoke**.（我停下来抽烟——为了抽烟而停下）

### 6. Reflexive Pronouns 反身代词

| 人称 | 反身代词 | 例句 |
|------|---------|------|
| I | **myself** | I taught **myself** English. |
| you (单) | **yourself** | Be careful! Don't hurt **yourself**. |
| he | **himself** | He cut **himself** while shaving. |
| she | **herself** | She looked at **herself** in the mirror. |
| it | **itself** | The door opened by **itself**. |
| we | **ourselves** | We enjoyed **ourselves** at the party. |
| you (复) | **yourselves** | Help **yourselves** to some cake. |
| they | **themselves** | They built the house **themselves**. |

> **两种用法**：
> 1. **强调作用**：I did it **myself**.（我自己做的，不是别人）
> 2. **反射作用**：动作回到主语：She taught **herself**.（她自学）

### 7. Reported Speech 间接引语

| 直接引语 | 间接引语（时态后移） |
|---------|-------------------|
| "I **am** tired." | He said he **was** tired. |
| "I **work** here." | She said she **worked** there. |
| "I **am working**." | She said she **was working**. |
| "I **have finished**." | He said he **had finished**. |
| "I **went** to Paris." | She said she **had gone** to Paris. |
| "I **will** call you." | He said he **would** call me. |
| "**Can** you help me?" | She asked if I **could** help her. |
| "**Do** you like it?" | He asked if I **liked** it. |

> **间接引语的词序变化**：
>
> | 直接引语 | 间接引语 |
> |---------|---------|
> | here | there |
> | this | that |
| now | then |
| today | that day |
| yesterday | the day before / the previous day |
| tomorrow | the next day / the following day |

### 8. so/such...that 结构

| 结构 | 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| so + 形容词/副词 + that | 如此……以至于 | The box was **so heavy that** I couldn't lift it. |
| so + many/few + 可数名词 + that | 如此多/少……以至于 | There were **so many people that** we couldn't find a seat. |
| so + much/little + 不可数名词 + that | 如此多/少……以至于 | He had **so much work that** he stayed up all night. |
| such + (a/an) + 形容词 + 名词 + that | 如此……以至于 | She is **such a kind person that** everyone likes her. |
| such + 形容词 + 复数名词 + that | 同上（复数） | They are **such beautiful flowers that** I bought them. |

> **so vs. such 快速区分**：
> - so + 形容词/副词：**so hot** that...
> - such + (a/an) + 形容词 + 名词：**such a hot day** that...
> - 但：so + many/much + 名词

### 9. too...to 结构

| 结构 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| too + 形容词/副词 + to do | 太……而不能 | He is **too young to go** to school. |
| too + 形容词 + for + 名词/代词 + to do | 对某人来说太……而不能 | The problem is **too difficult for me to solve**. |

> **与 enough 对比**：
> - **too...to**（太……而不能，否定意义）：He is **too short to reach** the shelf.
> - **enough...to**（足够……能，肯定意义）：He is **tall enough to reach** the shelf.

> **改写**：too...to = so...that + 否定
> - He is **too tired to work**. = He is **so tired that he cannot work**.> 💡 **使用建议**：本附录适合作为快速查阅工具。如遇不明之处，可结合教材各单元的具体讲解深入理解。建议在学习过程中，遇到生疏的语法点时随时翻阅本参考，并用自己的话仿写例句以加深记忆。*本附录对应《Family Album USA》全书语法要点，按教材编排顺序整理。*[← 附录C. 美国文化与社会习俗](appendix/sections/american-culture.md) | → 结尾
