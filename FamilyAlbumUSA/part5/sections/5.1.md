# 5.1 第十六集：The Community Center## 一、剧情概要 (Plot Summary)

The Stewart family gets involved with their local community center. Grandpa and Robbie visit the center to see what activities are available. They meet several people who run programs for seniors and children. The center is a busy place that offers many services to the community — from exercise classes for older adults to after-school programs for kids.

Marilyn, who is a talented photographer, offers to take photos for the center's promotional materials. Susan helps organize a fundraising event. The whole family volunteers their time and skills to support the community. Through their participation, the Stewarts discover that giving back to the community is both rewarding and fun.

**Key Plot Points:**
- Robbie and Grandpa visit the community center for the first time
- They learn about various programs that the center offers
- The family volunteers for different activities
- Marilyn contributes her photography skills
- The community hosts a fundraising event
- Everyone feels good about helping others## 二、经典对话 (Classic Dialogues)

### Dialogue 1: Visiting the Community Center

> **Grandpa:** This is quite a place! I didn't know we had such a big community center.
>
> **Director:** Welcome! We have activities for people of all ages. This is a place **that** welcomes everyone.
>
> **Robbie:** What kind of programs do you offer?
>
> **Director:** We have exercise classes for seniors, art workshops for children, and job-training programs for adults.
>
> **Grandpa:** That's wonderful. My granddaughter Susan, **who** is a teacher, might be able to help with the children's programs.
>
> **Director:** That would be great! We're always looking for volunteers **who** want to make a difference.

### Dialogue 2: Offering to Help

> **Marilyn:** I heard you need photos for your brochure.
>
> **Volunteer Coordinator:** Yes! We're looking for someone **who** can take professional-quality pictures.
>
> **Marilyn:** I can do that. I'm a photographer, and I'd love to contribute.
>
> **Volunteer Coordinator:** That's perfect! We need photos **that** show the center's activities — the exercise class, the children's art room, the senior lunch program.
>
> **Marilyn:** I'll bring my camera tomorrow. Is there anything else **that** you need help with?
>
> **Volunteer Coordinator:** Actually, we could use someone **who** can organize the annual fundraiser.
>
> **Marilyn:** I know just the person — my sister-in-law Susan!

### Dialogue 3: After the Fundraiser

> **Susan:** What a successful event! I'm so proud of everyone **who** helped.
>
> **Grandpa:** The community really came together. Look at all the money **that** we raised for the new playground.
>
> **Robbie:** And the best part is seeing all the people **who** will benefit from this.
>
> **Grandpa:** That's what community is all about. When you help others, you help yourself too.
>
> **Susan:** I think this is just the beginning. Our family should keep volunteering here.
>
> **Robbie:** I agree. There are so many ways **that** we can make a difference.## 三、核心词汇 (Core Vocabulary)

### 定语从句 — That / Which / Who

定语从句用来修饰一个名词或代词，充当定语的功能。引导定语从句的关系代词有 **that**、**which**、**who** 等。

#### 1. Who (指人)
"Who" 用来修饰人，在从句中作主语或宾语。

| 用法 | 例句 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 作主语 | *She is the woman **who** runs the program.* | who + 动词 |
| 作宾语 | *He is the volunteer **who** I met yesterday.* | who + 主语 + 动词 |

**更多例句：**
- *The people **who** volunteer here are very kind.* (在这里做志愿者的人很友善。)
- *I know a teacher **who** can help with the children.* (我认识一位可以帮助孩子们的老师。)

#### 2. Which (指物)
"Which" 用来修饰事物，在从句中作主语或宾语。

| 用法 | 例句 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 作主语 | *The center **which** opened last year is very popular.* | which + 动词 |
| 作宾语 | *The program **which** we started is going well.* | which + 主语 + 动词 |

**更多例句：**
- *The fundraiser **which** raised $5,000 was a success.* (筹得5000美元的筹款活动很成功。)
- *This is the brochure **which** Marilyn designed.* (这是玛丽莲设计的小册子。)

#### 3. That (指人或物)
"That" 既可以指人也可以指物，在口语中比 which 和 who 更常用。

| 用法 | 例句 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 指人 | *She is the volunteer **that** helps seniors.* | that + 动词 |
| 指物 | *This is the activity **that** everyone likes.* | that + 主语 + 动词 |

**更多例句：**
- *The center **that** we visited is very modern.* (我们参观的那个中心很现代化。)
- *He is the person **that** organized the event.* (他就是组织活动的那个人。)

#### 4. 用法对比总结

| 关系代词 | 指代对象 | 句子中的功能 | 例句 |
|----------|----------|--------------|------|
| **who** | 人 | 主语/宾语 | *the woman **who** volunteers* |
| **which** | 物 | 主语/宾语 | *the program **which** helps kids* |
| **that** | 人/物 | 主语/宾语 | *the center **that** serves everyone* |

> ⚠️ **注意：** 在非限制性定语从句中（有逗号分隔），只能用 who（指人）或 which（指物），不能用 that。
> - ✅ *My sister, **who** is a teacher, helps at the center.*
> - ❌ *My sister, **that** is a teacher, helps at the center.*## 六、文化注释 (Cultural Notes)

### 美国社区文化 (American Community Culture)

社区中心（**Community Center**）是美国社会生活的重要组成部分。几乎所有社区都有自己的社区中心，提供各种服务和活动。

**社区中心的主要功能：**
- 为各年龄段提供活动场所
- 组织教育和培训项目
- 举办社区聚会和庆祝活动
- 提供社会服务支援

### 志愿服务精神 (Volunteerism)

志愿服务（**Volunteerism**）是美国文化中根深蒂固的传统。根据美国劳工统计局的数据，每年有超过6000万美国人参与各种形式的志愿服务。

**美国志愿服务的几个特点：**

1. **人人参与** — 无论年龄、职业、收入水平，美国人普遍认为回馈社会是公民责任。剧中的Stewart一家就是典型例子。

2. **多样化形式** — 志愿服务不限于捐钱。可以像Marilyn一样贡献专业技能，也可以像Susan一样组织活动，或者像Robbie一样单纯付出时间。

3. **宗教与社区纽带** — 许多志愿服务通过教堂、社区中心、学校和慈善组织进行。

4. **从小培养** — 美国学校鼓励学生参与社区服务，许多高中甚至要求一定的志愿服务时数才能毕业。

5. **"Time, Talent, and Treasure"** — 美国人常用这个短语来表达三种回馈方式：时间、才能和金钱。

**经典表达：**
> "Ask not what your country can do for you — ask what you can do for your country."
> — John F. Kennedy (约翰·肯尼迪)

这句话体现了美国志愿服务精神的核心理念：主动贡献，而非被动索取。

### 本集中体现的志愿服务价值：

| 价值 | 体现 |
|------|------|
| 个人责任感 | 每个家庭成员主动提出帮忙 |
| 技能贡献 | Marilyn用摄影技能服务他人 |
| 社区凝聚力 | 通过志/愿活动建立联系 |
| 代际互助 | 老年人帮助孩子，年轻人帮助老年人 |## 七、自测 (Self-Test)
